Exploded Estimated Costs

The Exploded Estimated Costs window displays all estimated expenses for the project. To view the window:

  1. From the Info menu, select Exploded Estimated Costs. The Exploded Estimated Costs window appears.

  2. The window displays costs and hours for all operations, purchased parts, fabricated parts, and requirements.

  3. To limit the information shown in the window, select the View menu and click the type of information you would like to view. You can limit your view to operations only, purchased parts only, fabricated parts only, and requirements only.

  4. Click the X button to close the window.
