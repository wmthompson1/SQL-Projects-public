Viewing & Setting Customer Terms and Currency IDs in the Monetary Tab

  1. With the Customer ID for whom you want to view and set default Terms information appearing in the Customer ID field, click the Monetary tab.

The customer’s default terms appear in the field; the customer’s currency appears beneath the field.

  1. Click the Terms button to edit customer information.

The Terms dialog box appears.

  1. When you are finished setting Customer Term information, click the Ok button to commit the settings.

  2. If the customer requires a currency change, click the arrow and select a Currency ID from the list box.
