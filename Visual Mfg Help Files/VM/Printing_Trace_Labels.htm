Printing Trace Labels

Unless you are relying on preassigned vendor lot or serial numbers that are marked on the materials or their containers, you probably want to print trace labels. You can only do this after completing the transaction, because VISUAL does not assign auto-numbers until that time.

Trace Labels are printed for all traceable parts on the receiver, rather than a line at a time.

To print trace labels:

  1. Click the Print Trace Labels button.

The Print Part Trace Labels dialog appears, displaying the Customer Order ID, the customer name, and the Packlist ID.

  1. Select the label format.

You can choose from the standard label format, or one of three user-defined formats. The user-defined formats are only be available if the form has been defined.

  1. Click Ok.

A standard Windows Print dialog box appears.

  1. Make any appropriate selections and click Ok to print the labels.
