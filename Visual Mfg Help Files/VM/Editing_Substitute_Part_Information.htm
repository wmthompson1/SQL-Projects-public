Editing Substitute Part Information

To edit substitute part information, call the appropriate part into the Part Maintenance window, click the Order Management tab and then click the Substitute Parts button. Double-click <Substitute Part ID> to view the part table and select a new Part ID. Modify the Rank of a substitute part as necessary by placing your cursor in the Rank field and making the necessary change.

Take note that you cannot enter new Part IDs in the Substitute Parts dialog box. To enter new parts, refer to Adding Parts to the Database.

Refer to Editing Part Information for more on modifying parts in inventory.
