Starting the Purchase Management Window

To start the Purchase Management Window:

From VISUAL’s Purchasing menu, select the Purchase Management Window option.

The Purchase Management Window appears.

The Purchase Order Entry window appears as a child window within the Purchase Management Window. Notice that the Purchase Order Entry toolbar appears in the Purchase Management toolbar and not the Purchase Order Entry child window.

Because the Purchase Order Entry window is an integral part of the Purchase Management Window, when you close the Purchase Order Entry Window, the Purchase Management Window also closes.
