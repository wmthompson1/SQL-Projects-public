Deactivating Service Charges

If you no longer use a service charge, you can deactivate it. When a service charge is deactivated, users cannot specify it on any new records or transactions. Inactive service charges are not displayed in service charge drop-down lists or browse tables, and users cannot manually specify an inactive service charge in a Service Charge field. Inactive service charges used on existing records are not removed.

To deactivate a service charge:

  1. Select Maintain, Service Charge Defaults.

  2. Select the ID of the service charge to be deactivated.

  3. Clear the Active check box.

  4. Click Save.
