Assigning Purchase Order Line Quantities to Demand

You can assign purchase order quantities to five different sources of demand. The five sources of demand to which you can assign purchase order line quantities are:

Customer Order Delivery Schedules

Customer Orders

Interbranch Transfers

Work Order Material Requirements

Inventory (Warehouse)
