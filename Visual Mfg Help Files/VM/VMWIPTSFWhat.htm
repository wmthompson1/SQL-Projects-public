What Is WIP Inventory Transaction Entry?

Use the WIP Inventory Transaction window to adjust materials into WIP locations, adjust materials out of WIP locations, and transfer materials between WIP locations.

You can also use the WIP Inventory Transaction window to review WIP Inventory transactions. WIP Inventory transactions are generated by these actions:

  • Delivering materials in the Material Handler Queue

  • Adjusting materials into WIP locations

  • Adjusting materials out of WIP locations

  • Transferring materials between WIP locations
