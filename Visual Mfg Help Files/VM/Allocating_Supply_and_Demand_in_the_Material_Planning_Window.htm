Allocating Supply and Demand in the Material Planning Window

Because the Material Planning Window contains a master list of all the orders in your database, you can perform the same allocations from this window that you can perform in the application through which you entered the original order. For example, if you want to allocate a portion of a purchase order line quantity to demand, rather than opening the Purchase Order Entry window and searching for the order, use the Material Planning Window to make the allocation.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, you can set up allocations on a site-by-site basis. You cannot allocate materials from one site to a second site.

You can perform these allocations in the Material Planning Window:

  • Allocate Supply to Work Order Material Demand

  • Allocate Supply to a Customer Order Demand

  • Allocate Supply to an Interbranch Transfer Demand

  • Allocate Work Order Supply to Demand

  • Allocate Purchase Order to Demand

  • Allocate Interbranch Transfers to Demand
