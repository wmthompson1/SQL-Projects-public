Editing Quote Line Items

You can add, edit, and delete individual line items of an existing quotation.

Adding Line Items

  1. With the appropriate quotation open in the Estimating Window, click the Insert button.

An arrow appears in the row header of the new line. A new line appears in the table.

  1. Enter the line item information.

  2. Click the Save button to update the quotation.

Editing Line Items

  1. With the appropriate quotation open in the Estimating Window, move the cursor to the field you want to edit. You cannot remove quantities quoted.

  2. Make the appropriate changes to the field.

A check mark appears in the row header.

  1. Click the Save button to update the quotation.

Deleting Line Items

  1. With the appropriate quotation open in the Estimating Window, highlight the line item that you want to delete.

  2. Click the Delete button at the bottom of the table (not the Delete button at the bottom of the window).

An "X" appears in the row header of the item you are deleting.

  1. Click the Save button to remove the item and update the quotation..
