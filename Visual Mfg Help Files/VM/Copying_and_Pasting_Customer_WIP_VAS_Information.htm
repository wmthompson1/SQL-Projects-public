Copying and Pasting Customer WIP/VAS Information

You can copy and paste information from one line to another. To copy information, select the line in the table you want to copy and click the Copy button.

To paste the information, select the line into which you want to paste the information and click the Paste button. The line must exist before you can paste into it. When you paste the information into the new line, the fields at the bottom of the dialog box fill in with the appropriate information.
