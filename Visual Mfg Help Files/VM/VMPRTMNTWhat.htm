What is Part Maintenance?

Use Part Maintenance to add, delete, and edit part information for all the parts in your company’s database. A part can be either the raw material or other purchased components necessary in the manufacturing process, or goods produced during the manufacturing process. A part can be either a finished piece or an intermediate assembly used in another process. The process can be an operation on a different leg of the same work order or a completely different work order. The latter involves a receipt from one work order and an issue to another while the former does not.

The information you enter here appears and is used in many other parts of the system.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, some of the part information is specified at the tenant level and some of the information is specified at the site level.
