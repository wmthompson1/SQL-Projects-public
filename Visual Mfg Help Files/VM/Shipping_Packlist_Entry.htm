Shipping Packlist Entry

The ability to ship goods from inventory can be tremendously improved through barcode scanning. Goods can be shipped to any order from any location and the final packlist printed within the standard VISUAL Shipping Entry window.

The chain of events is similar to the following:

  • Pack List is printed

  • Material is located within Warehouse/Location

  • (optional) Shipping labels are printed

  • Material is placed on delivery system

  • Material is scanned to update Inventory and applied to Shipping Totals for Customer Order

Shipping Labels

Shipping labels are useful for shipping transactions as well as providing compliance labeling for customers. These provide a method for tracking inventory as it leaves a facility.
