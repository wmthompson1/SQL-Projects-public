Behind the Scenes of the Allocation Process

The VISUAL Allocation utility allocates supply to demand in three passes:

  1. Attempt to allocate on hand inventory to demand orders in rank (priority), due date order.

If supply is sufficient and committed to all demand orders, allocation is complete. If not, pass 2 and 3 are still available.

  1. Allocate on hand inventory to demand in rank (priority), due date order, up to the fill rate on the order.

  2. Allocate remaining on hand inventory and future supply orders to demand orders in rank (priority), due date order until supply runs out.

Orders that received fill rate allocation in pass two are eligible to receive future supply allocation in this pass.
