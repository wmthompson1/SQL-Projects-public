Purchase Price History

In the Part Purchase History dialog box you can view the following information for each line:

Order Quantity - The amount you order.

Received Quantity - The amount you receive.

Currency - The type of currency you use.

Unit Price - The price per unit.

Discount % - The part discount percent.

Fixed Cost - The one time cost from the purchase order.

Standard Unit Cost - The part cost from Part Maintenance.

You can sort the Part Purchase History table by Order Date, Desired Recv Date, or Purchase Order, and sequence the Purchase Orders in ascending or descending order.

Select a line and click the View button to inspect a purchase order that corresponds to a line in the table.

The Purchase Order window appears with the selected purchase order. You can now view any other details of the Purchase Order that do not appear in the table, including notations and specifications. If you are currently editing or creating a Purchase Order, VISUAL prompts you to confirm the new or changed information before VISUAL resets the window.
