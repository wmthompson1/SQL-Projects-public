What is Audit Maintenance?

For publicly traded companies, United States federal law requires companies to meet SOX or Sarbox compliance, as of July 30, 2002. The Sarbanes-Oxley Act establishes new or enhanced operating standards for U.S. companies. While the federal government requires this compliance of public company boards, management, and public accounting firms, it does not apply to private companies. VISUAL provides functionality to maintain historical data, whether required by the government or not.

Audit Maintenance does not ensure SOX compliance on its own, however. You must implement the appropriate business practices to achieve compliance.

The scope of the Audit Maintenance function allows you to track any non-binary field in the database. However, Infor does not recommend that you track every field, as this places a strain on your database.
