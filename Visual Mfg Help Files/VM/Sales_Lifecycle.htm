Sales Lifecycle

You can access the Lifecycle Document Viewer in these Sales modules:

  • Estimating Window

  • Customer Order Entry

  • Order Management Window

  • Manufacturing Window

  • Return Material Authorization

  • Shipment Entry

  • AR Invoices

  • AR Payments
