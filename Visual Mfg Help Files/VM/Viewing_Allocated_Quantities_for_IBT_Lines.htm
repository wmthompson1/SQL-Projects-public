Viewing Allocated Quantities for IBT Lines

After you allocate supply to an IBT line, you can view allocation information on the IBT line in the line item table.

The following two columns display important allocation information:

Demand Quantity Allocated - The quantity of parts that you have allocated to the line from a supplying sources. This quantity can never be greater that the quantity of the line.

The quantity in this column less the order quantity of the line is the quantity available to you to which you can still allocate supply.

Demand Quantity Fulfilled - The quantity of parts that the supplying source has fulfilled.
