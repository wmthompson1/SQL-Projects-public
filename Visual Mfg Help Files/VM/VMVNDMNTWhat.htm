What is Vendor Maintenance?

Use Vendor Maintenance to enter and maintain the vendor database. You do not need to enter all of the information requested in the Vendor Maintenance window, but the more complete the window the better the system is be able to fill in information in other parts of the system. The information you enter here appears and is used in many other parts of the system.
