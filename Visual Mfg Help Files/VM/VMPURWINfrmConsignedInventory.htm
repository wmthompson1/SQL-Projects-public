Using the Consigned Inventory Tool

Use the Consigned Inventory child window to track your consigned inventories.

The Consigned Inventory View has three levels: Consign Type, Warehouse/Location, and Part.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, the Consigned Inventory tool shows information for all of your sites.

To view consigned inventory:

  1. Select the Consigned Inventory option from the Tools menu.

The Consigned Inventory tool opens, populated with your consignment types.

  1. Select what you want to appear in the child window. You can filter what appears in the Consigned Inventory child window based on the following settings:

Customer Warehouses – To view inventory you store at your customer’s site, select the Customer Warehouses check box.

Vendor Warehouses – To view inventory you store at your vendor’s location, select the Vendor Warehouses check box.

Customer Locations – To view your customer’s inventory stored at your facility, select the Customer Locations check box.

Vendor Locations – To view your vendor’s inventory stored at your facility, select the Vendor Locations check box.

At Or Below Order Point – If you want VISUAL to include parts with quantities that are at or below their set order points , select the At Or Below Order Point check box.

Above Order Up To Qty – If you want VISUAL to include parts with quantities that are above their order points, select the Above Order Up To Qty check box.

Selecting any of these options in the Consigned Inventory window overrides the corresponding selections on the Consigned Inventory tab of the Search Criteria window.

  1. After changing your viewing options, click the Refresh button. VISUAL re-populates the Consigned Inventory tool, applying the options you selected.

Viewing Consignment Warehouses and Locations

To view warehouse and location information, double-click Consigned Inventory and select the consignment type you want to view.

Viewing Consignment Part Information

To view part information for your consignment warehouses and locations, in the explorer panel, click the warehouse in which you are interested.

If you expand the Warehouse/Location all of the consigned parts appear below the warehouse name.

Creating Purchase Orders for Consigned Inventory

When at the part level, you can create purchase orders for the parts appearing in the Consigned Inventory child window.

Use the following methods to create purchase orders:

  • Select one or more part rows and drag them onto the Purchase Order History child window. VISUAL creates purchase orders for all of the parts you selected, consolidating the orders by vendor.

  • Select one or more part rows and press the Generate P/Os button. VISUAL generates the purchase orders and a message appears indicating that VISUAL has created a quantity of purchase orders. You can only generate POs for “from Vendor” type consignments.

  • Select one or more part rows and drag them onto the Purchase Order Entry child window. VISUAL adds lines to the purchase order table. You can then review and modify them before saving the order.

For more information, refer to the Releasing Purchase Orders section.
