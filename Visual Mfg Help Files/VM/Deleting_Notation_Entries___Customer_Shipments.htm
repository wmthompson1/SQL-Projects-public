Deleting Notation Entries – Customer Shipments

To delete an entry:

  1. Use the navigation arrows to the left of the dialog box to move to the entry you want to delete.

  2. Click the Del button.

A note appears next to the entry stating that the entry is marked for deletion.

  1. Click the Save button to remove the entry.

  2. Click the Close button to close the Notations dialog box and return to the Customer Shipments window.
