Entering and Maintaining Unplanned Maintenance Information

Before you can begin using the Unplanned Maintenance window, you must enter some basic information for equipment and services.

From the Maintain menu, you can enter and maintain information for the following:

  • Fault Types

  • Fault Codes

  • Remedy Types

  • Remedy Codes

  • Urgency Codes

  • Escalation Codes

  • Engineer Assignments

  • Standard Repairs
