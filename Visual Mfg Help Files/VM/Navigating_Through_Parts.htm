Navigating Through Parts

After you have called a part into the Material Planning Window, you can navigate among all parts in the search list by using the left-arrow and right-arrow buttons to the left of the Part ID field.

Press the right-arrow button to automatically view the next part in the search list.

Press the left-arrow button view the previous part.

When you view the first part, VISUAL moves the part list behind the Material Planning Window, but it is still open. You can always return to this list window to select another part, or to print a report.
