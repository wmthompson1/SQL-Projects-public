Deleting Part Aliases

  1. With the part for which you want to establish part aliases displaying in the Part ID field in the Part Maintenance window, click the Part Aliases button from the Order Management tab.

  2. Highlight the part alias line that you want to delete and click the Delete button. VISUAL places an X in the line’s left-most column.

  3. Click the Save button to complete the deletion.
