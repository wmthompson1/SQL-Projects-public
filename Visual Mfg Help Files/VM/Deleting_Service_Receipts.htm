Deleting Service Receipts

To delete Service Receipts:

  1. Call the service receipt you want to delete into the Service Receipt Entry window.

  2. Click the Delete button.

A dialog box appears, asking you to confirm the deletion.

  1. Click Yes to confirm the deletion, or No to cancel.

If you click Yes, the Service Receipt Entry window still contains the information, but the receipt has been removed from the database.

  1. Click the Clear button to clear the transaction from the window.

If you click the Save button at this point, VISUAL recreates the transaction you just deleted.
