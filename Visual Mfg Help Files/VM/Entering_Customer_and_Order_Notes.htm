Entering Customer and Order Notes

The customer notes and order notes are basically the same. The difference between them is that customer notes are tied to the customer and order notes are tied to the order. That is, a note you enter for a customer in one order also appears in every other order that the customer places. The notes you enter for an order only appear in the order in which they were written.

  1. With the appropriate customer open in the Customer Order Entry window, click the Order Notes or Cust Notes button.

The Notations dialog box appears.

  1. Click the New button to make a new entry.

A line appears with the current date and time for the entry.

  1. Enter the appropriate information.

You can enter an unlimited amount of information.

  1. Use the Paste button to paste text that you copied from another location into the box.

  2. Use the Copy button to copy text in the dialog box to the clipboard.

  3. Click the Save button to save the Notes.

VISUAL adds the user name to the end of the notation entry.

  1. Click the Close button to close the Notations dialog box and return to the Customer Order Entry window.
