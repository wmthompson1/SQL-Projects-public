Starting Unit of Measure Maintenance

Unit of Measure Maintenance is available from the Maintenance menu of the Infor VISUAL Main window.

To access the Unit of Measure Maintenance window:

Select Unit of Measure Maintenance from the Inventory menu.

The Unit of Measure Maintenance screen appears with the units of measure that are already established.

The Unit of Measure Maintenance window contains upper and lower sections. The upper is the FROM section and the lower is the TO section. You enter all information in the TO section of the window.
