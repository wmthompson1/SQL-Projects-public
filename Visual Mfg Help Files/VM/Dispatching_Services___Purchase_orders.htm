Dispatching Services – Purchase Orders

You can dispatch parts to an outside service using the Purchase Order Entry Window.

  1. Enter the Order ID for the service in the Our Order ID field.

  2. Select the Service Line you want to dispatch parts to.

  3. Enter the number of parts you want to dispatch in the Quantity Dispatch column.

  4. Click the Save button.

VISUAL dispatches the parts and the number of parts dispatched appears in the Total Quantity Dispatched column.
