Assigning Tasks

To assign tasks:

  • In Purchase Requisition Entry, assign a user in the Assigned To field or assign a user or group to one of the four approval fields on the Approvals tab. See Initiating Purchase Requisitions.

  • In ECN Entry, assign a user in the Assigned To field or assign a user or group to one of the teams on the ECN header. See Entering Basic Information into ECNs.

  • In Workflow, create a rule that consists of a task. Assign users or groups to the task. See Adding Rule Properties.
