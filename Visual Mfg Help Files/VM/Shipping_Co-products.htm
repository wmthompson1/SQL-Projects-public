Shipping Co-products

Use Shipping Entry to ship co-products. Similar to receiving parts into inventory, VISUAL handles the shipping process for each part on the work order separately.

  1. Select Shipping Entry from the Tools menu of the VISUAL Main window.

The Customer Shipments window appears.

  1. In the Order ID field, enter the customer order that contains the co-products you want to ship.

The customer information for the order appears in the Customer Shipments window. The line items show all the parts for the customer order.

  1. Highlight the line that contains the co-product you want to ship.

  2. Click in the Ship Quantity column and enter the quantity of the part you are shipping.

If the quantity you are shipping is less than the total quantity ordered for that part, a dialog box appears, asking if you want to close the line’s remaining balance.

Click Yes to close the line or No to keep the line open.

  1. Enter the appropriate shipping information.

  2. Click Save to ship the part.
