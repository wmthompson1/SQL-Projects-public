Allocating Purchase Orders to Demand

With the purchase order from which you want to allocate supply to demand highlighted, select Allocations and then Allocate Purchase Order to Demand from the Edit menu. The Assign to Demand dialog box appears.

You can allocate purchase order supply to these demand sources:

  • Customer Order Delivery Schedule demand

  • Customer Order demand

  • Inventory demand

  • Work Order Supply

  • Work Order Material Requirement supply

  • Interbranch Transfer supply
