Converting Replace RMAs

You can convert a Replace RMA to a Credit RMA or Repair RMA. These conditions apply to the conversions:

  • If you created a new order during the Replace RMA process and received the returned materials, you must use this new order during the Credit or Repair RMA process.

  • When you convert a Replace RMA to a Credit RMA, the associated customer order is closed when you receive shipment of the returned materials.

  • When you convert a Replace RMA to a Repair RMA, you may need to manually create the Repair work order.
