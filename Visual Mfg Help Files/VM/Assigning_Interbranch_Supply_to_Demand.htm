Assigning Interbranch Supply to Demand

As you can assign supply to outgoing IBT lines, you can in turn assign outgoing IBTs to demand.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, you can assign a quantity to demand for transactions entered in the To Warehouse ID’s site. For example, if the warehouse you specified in the to warehouse site belongs to site MMC-SITE B, then only transactions created in MMC-SITE B are eligible for selection in the Assign to Demand dialog box.

Select from these four supply options when allocating IBT line supply to demand:

Customer Order Delivery Schedule supply

Customer Order supply

Work Order Material Requirement supply

Interbranch Transfer supply
