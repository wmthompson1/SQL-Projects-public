Viewing Shipping Performance

Use Shipping Performance to view purchasing and receiving information for a part.

In the Part Purchase History dialog box you can view the following information for each line:

Order Date - The date you ordered the part.

Desired Recv Date - The date you want to receive the part.

Promise Date - The date the vendor promises to deliver the part to you.

Last Actual Recv Date - The date you actually receive the part.
