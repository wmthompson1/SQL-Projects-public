Generating Supply Orders

The Material Planning Window is designed to be the single application for all your material planning needs. One of its most useful features is to use the information displayed in this window to automatically generate supply orders without having to explicitly enter all the order information.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, place supply orders on a site-by-site basis. If you are licensed to use a single site, place supply orders on an enterprise-wide basis.
