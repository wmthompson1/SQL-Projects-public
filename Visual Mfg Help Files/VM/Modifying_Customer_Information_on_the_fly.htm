Modifying Customer Information on the fly

You can click the Customer Maintenance button to view customer information for the current customer and make any necessary changes.

  1. Using the Customer Name or Customer ID button, select the customer for whom you are placing the order.

  2. Click the Customer Maintenance button.

The Customer Maintenance window appears, displaying the current customer’s information.

  1. Modify any customer information.

  2. When you are finished making changes, click the Save button.
