Deleting Purchase Requisitions

Under most circumstances, you can delete a purchase requisition from the database. There are, however, prohibiting factors that limit your ability to delete a requisition with a status of "Ordered" or "Cancelled/Void." Ordered requisitions have progressed through the approval stage to become actual purchase orders. Until they are successfully closes, VISUAL guards against deletion.

Requisitions with any other status can effectively be removed from the database.

Rather than delete a requisition, change its status to Cancelled/Void to keep the record in the database while preventing further changes to it.

  1. If you are licensed to use multiple sites, click the Site ID arrow and select the site that contains the requisition. If you are licensed to use a single site, this field is unavailable.

  2. With the appropriate purchase requisition open in the Purchase Requisition Entry window, click the Delete button on the main toolbar.

A dialog box appears, informing you that VISUAL will delete the purchase requisition if you continue.

  1. Click the Yes button to permanently remove the purchase requisition from the database or the No button to quit the procedure.

    As mentioned above, if the requisition has a status of Closed, you cannot delete it. VISUAL permits deletion of all other statuses.

  1. Click the Save toolbar button to complete the deletion.
