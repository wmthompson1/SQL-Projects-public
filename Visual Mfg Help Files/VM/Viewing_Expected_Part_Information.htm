Viewing Expected Part Information

Expected part information is one level down the Part Availability tree.

  1. In the Part Availability window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window. The structure expands to the next level.

  2. Highlight Expected.

The Detail information appears in the right pane as follows:

P/O - The quantity of the specified part that you are expecting to receive through purchase orders.

WO - The quantity of the specified part that you expect to receive through work order production.

IBT - The quantity of the specified part that you expect to receive through interbranch transfers.

Unallocated Demand - The quantity that you have yet to allocate from the above three supplying sources.

Click the button to view details for the appropriate type.
