Referencing Header Documents and Line Documents

Purchase Requisition Entry supports the ability to link documents to both headers and lines. Using Document Maintenance, you can maintain a master list of systems documents to use throughout your VISUAL system. After creating a document base, link individual records, such as purchase requisitions, to these centralized documents, over which you can maintain strict version control to ensure accuracy across the full range of your enterprise.

Within Purchase Requisition Entry, you can specify multiple documents per header and multiple documents per line, if applicable.
