Using a Right Mouse Click to Allocate Available to Customer Supply to Customer Orders

You can also right click Available to Customer Supply to allocate supply to the current customer order.

To perform this operation, you must have the Current Customer Order window and Part Availability window open. The part that you want to allocate to the customer order line must be displaying in the Part Availability window.

  1. In the Part Availability window, double-click the icon and highlight Available to Customer.

  2. Right-click the line. An Allocate message box appears.

  3. Left-click the message box to allocate the supply.

VISUAL allocates inventory supply from the appropriate customer warehouse to the customer order line. In the Current Customer Order window, a plus box appears next to the line to which you allocated supply. If you click this plus box, the ID of the Warehouse from which you allocated the supply appears.
