Viewing Committed Part Information

Committed part information is one level down the Part Availability tree.

  1. In the Part Availability window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window. The structure expands to the next level.

  2. Highlight Committed.

The Detail information that appears in the right pane is as follows:

C/O - The quantity of the specified part that you have committed through customer orders.

Req (Requirement) - The quantity of the specified part that you have committed through work order material requirements.

IBT - The quantity of the specified part that you have committed through interbranch transfers.

Unallocated Demand - The quantity that you have yet to allocate to the above three demand sources.

Click the appropriate button to view details for each type.
