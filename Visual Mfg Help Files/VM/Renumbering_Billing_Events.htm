Renumbering Billing Events

For example, you have lines 10, 20, 30, 40, and 50 and you want to move line 50 to appear between lines 10 and 20.

To change the order in which your event lines appear:

  1. Select the line you want to move.

  2. Click in the Seq # field and enter a number between the sequence numbers where you want to move the line.

In the example, select line 50 and change its number to anything between 10 and 20.

  1. Click the Renumber button.

  2. When VISUAL prompts you to save the changes, click the Save button.

VISUAL changes line 50’s Seq # to 20 and renumbers all other lines in increments of 10 keeping them in the same order.
