Viewing Test Step Definition History

You can view the history of test step definitions in Test Definition.

  1. Highlight the test step line item for which you want to view the history.

  2. Click the History button.

The Test Step History dialog box for the test appears.

From the Test Step History dialog box you can view test results, capabilities, exceptions, and graphs and histograms of test results by clicking on the appropriate buttons.

  1. When you are finished viewing the history information, click the Close button.
