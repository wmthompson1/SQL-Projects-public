Defining Co-products

You can define co-product parts manufactured on this work order. You can define quantities and percentage of cost.

  1. Click the Co-Products button.

The Co-products dialog box appears.

  1. Click the Insert button to add a new co-product.

A new line appears in the line item table.

  1. Enter the Part ID for the co-product.

  2. Enter the percentages and the quantity in the appropriate columns.

  3. Click the Save button to save the new co-product.
