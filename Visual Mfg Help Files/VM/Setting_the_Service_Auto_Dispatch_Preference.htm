Setting the Service Auto Dispatch Preference

  1. From the Options menu of the Purchase Order Entry Window, select Preferences.

The Preferences dialog box appears.

  1. Select the Auto Dispatch Services check box.

  2. Click the Ok button to commit this preference.

With this preference set, as you enter service purchase order lines, the order quantity automatically becomes the Dispatch Quantity as well; after you save the purchase order, the order quantity becomes the Total Quantity Dispatched.
