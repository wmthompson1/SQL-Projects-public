Starting Equipment Maintenance

Equipment Maintenance is available from the Equipment Maintenance menu.

To open the window, select Equipment Maintenance from the Equip Maint menu.

The Equipment Maintenance window appears.

The Equipment Maintenance window is divided into a header section and a tabbed section. Don’t concern yourself with any of the fields in the window yet. Begin using the window by entering general equipment information. See the next section for more information.
