Viewing Expected Part Information for Purchase Orders

You can view information regarding parts that you expect from purchase orders two levels down on the Part Availability tree.

  1. In the Part Availability window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window. The structure expands to the next level.

  2. Highlight Expected and then click the plus box next to it. Double-click Expected to avoid clicking the plus box. The structure again expands.

  3. Highlight Expected PO. Purchase orders for that part appear in the right pane. You can maximize the window and view the detail information in the right pane or click the Expected PO plus box to expand the tree to the next level, where the detail information appears in both panes.

The Detail information that appears in the right pane as follows:

Supply ID - The ID of the purchase order.

Date - The date on which you placed the purchase order.

Order Quantity - The quantity of the purchase order line.

Received - The quantity of the line that you have received

Allocated - The portion of the order quantity that you have allocated to demand. You must have received the purchase order to fulfill the allocation.

Unallocated Demand - The quantity, if any, that you have yet to allocate from the purchase order to demand.
