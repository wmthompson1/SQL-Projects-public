Opening and Loading Views into the Customer Order History Child Window

Use the Customer Order History child window to view a customer’s order history, instantly recall and open a customer order, and append and copy an entire order or a line from an order to the current order. Because customers may often order the same parts or materials, the copy/append feature makes order entry a good deal easier by allowing you to transfer existing customer defaults and preferences to new orders.

  1. From the Tools menu, select Customer Order History.

The Customer Order History window appears.

If you have an order open in the Order Management Window, the Customer Order History window opens displaying order history for the customer in the open order. If you don’t have an order called into the screen, select a customer to view.

  1. To select a customer, click the Options button on the main toolbar, click the Customer tab, and then use the Customer ID button to select a customer.

The following columns appear in the right pane of the window:

Order ID - The Order ID of the customer order. Every order for this specific customer that exists in your database appears in this column.

Order Date - The date on which the customer placed the order.

Customer P/O ID - The customer’s ID of the purchase order for the order.

Status - The status of the customer order: Firmed, Released, Closed, Cancelled/Void, or On Hold. Select a status at the time of order entry.

Amount Ordered - The dollar amount of the entire order.

Amount Shipped - The quantity to the current date that you have shipped to the customer.

Territory - The customer’s default territory, as set in Customer Maintenance.

Shipping Status - The status of the customer order. Statuses include: Not Shipped & Late, Not Shipped & Not Late, Partially Shipped, Shipped, and Cancellation Before Shipment . Each status has a different colored icon. Click here for more information.

Carrier ID - The customer’s default carrier, from Customer Maintenance.
