Viewing Unavailable Part Information

Unavailable part information is one level down the Part Availability tree.

You must make assign parts in warehouse locations the status of unavailable in Warehouse Maintenance.

  1. In the Part Availability window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window. The structure expands to the next level.

  2. Highlight Unavailable

The detail information that appears in the right pane is the same as the information that appears for locked parts. See the above list for more specific information.
