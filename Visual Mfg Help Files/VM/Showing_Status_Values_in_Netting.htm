Showing Status Values in Netting

You can select the status values of demand and supply orders to be included in netting. Note that this does not just affect whether they are shown or not, but the netting itself.

You can choose various View menu options to include orders with:

  • Unreleased

  • Firmed

  • Closed

  • On Hold

  • Cancelled

Released orders are always shown. The Show Released Status option is permanently disabled in the Material Planning Window; it is there to remind you that this status is always included.

VISUAL considers customer orders with a status of Hold to be unreleased orders.
