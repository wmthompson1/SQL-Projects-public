Removing a Detail Grid

You can permanently remove a detail grid from the dashboard, or you can temporarily remove a detail grid.

To remove a detail grid from the dashboard:

  1. Select File, Dashboard.

  2. In the dashboard, select File, Open and select the dashboard that contains the detail grid.

  3. Click the Close button in the upper right corner of the detail grid.

  4. To permanently remove the detail grid from the dashboard, click Save.

To temporarily remove the detail grid from the dashboard, do not click Save. To redisplay the detail grid, click Restore.
