What Are Repair RMAs?

Use Repair RMAs when your company has agreed to receive the materials, diagnose and repair them, then reship them to the customer. Depending on the way you schedule the repairs, this may involve a work order. The new work order tracks the value being added to the part, not the original cost of the material.

Use these procedures to create and process Repair RMAs:

  • Creating Repair RMAs

  • Creating the Repair Work Order

  • Preparing the Initial Pricing for the Repair Work Order

  • Shipping the Repaired Materials

  • Approving Repair Pricing for Invoicing

  • Closing or Canceling Incomplete Repair RMAs

  • Costing Repair RMAs

  • Converting Repair RMAs
