Viewing Allocation Information for a Purchase Order Delivery Schedule Line

After you allocate P/O delivery schedule supply to a source of demand, you can view what quantity of the total delivery schedule line quantity you allocated.

The Quantity Allocated column in the Edit Delivery Schedule dialog box line item table contains the quantity of parts that you have allocated to demand. Until this value equals the quantity of the P/O delivery schedule line, you can still make allocations against the line.
