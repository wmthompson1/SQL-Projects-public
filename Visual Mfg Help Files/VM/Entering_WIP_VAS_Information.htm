Entering WIP/VAS Information

Before you can enter WIP/VAS information, you must select the WIP/VAS Enabled check box. If WIP/VAS is not enabled, none of the WIP/VAS constraints apply to allocation and the features for maintaining and using WIP/VAS are not visible.

WIP/VAS are customization services that you perform on components or inventory. While not completely manufacturing in nature, wip/vas requires the management of inventory moving and out service sections of a warehouse. WIP/VAS starts during order entry, when a customer requests one of these specialized services. WIP/VAS is only for warehouses/dcs with capability to manage specifications also available at line level.

  1. In the Maintenance section, click the WIP/VAS button. The WIP/VAS Maintenance dialog box appears.

  2. Click Insert to start adding line item information to the table.

WIP/VAS ID - An alphanumeric unique identifier of the service that you perform on a part.

Description - A description of the service.

Unit Price - The cost of the service per unit.

  1. Click Save when you are finished entering WIP/VAS information.
