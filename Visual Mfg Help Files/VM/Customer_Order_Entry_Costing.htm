Customer Order Entry

Customer Order Entry allows you to enter and process customer orders. You can assign a cost of goods accounts on the line item of the Customer Order. By selecting the line and then Cost of Goods Sold G/L Accounts from the Edit menu, you can cause the Material, Labor, Burden, and Service cost of goods to be assigned to the same or different accounts. That is, you can assign a different account to each of the cost of goods fields or you can assign one account in each of the fields. If you do not make an entry here for these accounts, VISUAL uses the cost of goods sold accounts from the General Ledger Account Interface Table.
