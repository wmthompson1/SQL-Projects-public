Entering Purchase Addresses

If a vendor uses purchase addresses that are different from their mailing address, enter them using this procedure. A vendor can have as many different purchase addresses as necessary. The steps below describe the procedure for adding purchase addresses.

  1. Select Maintain, Purchase Addresses.

  1. Click the Insert button.

A new line appears in the Purchase Addresses table.

  1. Specify the information for the purchase address in the fields at the bottom of the window.

The information automatically appears in the table as you tab through the address fields. You can also copy and paste information from one line to another.

  1. If you want to specify Intrastat information for the purchase address, click the Intrastat tab and specify this information:

Port of Transshipment - This is place from which the goods are shipped.

Country ID - Enter the country ID for the purchase address.

  1. Click Save to save the new information.

Searching For Purchase Addresses

You can search the VISUAL database for Purchase Addresses for a vendor. To do this, click the Search button.

A new line appears in the table.

To begin a search, enter search patterns to match in each of the fields of interest. You can use wildcards in these patterns.

There are two types of wildcard you can specify. Use the % character to indicate zero or more of any character in the position it is placed. Use the _ (underscore) character to indicate exactly one of any character in the position is it is placed. Any other characters must be matched exactly.

Deleting Purchase Addresses

To remove a purchase address completely, highlight the address line and click Delete.
