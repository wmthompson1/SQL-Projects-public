Assigning Employees to Sites

To add existing employees to a site:

  1. Select Maintain, Site Employees.

  2. Click the Site ID arrow and select the site to which you are adding employees.

  3. To add employees to the site, click the Add to Site.

To add all employees, click Select All for Add. To clear all selections in the Add to Site column, click Unselect All for Add.

  1. Click Save. After you click save, the Exists in Site check box is selected for the employees you added.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, each employee's pay rate is maintained by site. The same employee can have different pay rates in different sites. After you assign employees to your sites in Site Maintenance, go to Employee Maintenance to specify each employee's pay rate for each site..

You can also add employees to sites in Employee Maintenance.
