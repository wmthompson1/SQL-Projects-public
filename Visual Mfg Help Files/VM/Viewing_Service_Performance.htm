Viewing Service Performance

In the Service Purchase History dialog box you can view the following information for each line:

Order Date - The date you order the outside service.

Desired Recv Date

Promise Date - The date the outside vendor service promises to return the parts to you.

Last Dispatch Date - The last date you sent parts to the outside service vendor.

Last Received Date - The last date you received parts from the outside service vendor.
