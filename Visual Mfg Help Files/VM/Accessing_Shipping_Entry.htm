Accessing Shipping Entry

You can access Shipping Entry directly from the Customer Order Entry window. Right-click in the Sold To/Ship To address field in the Customer Order Entry header and select Shipping from the menu. If you selected a customer order in the Customer Order Entry window, the system inserts the details of the Customer order into the Shipping window. To activate the menu item, you must be viewing an order.

If you have already made a shipment against the order, you can also click the Shipping Entry lifecycle target button in the toolbar to open Shipping Entry. If you have not yet made a shipment for the order, then this button is not available. See Source and Target Buttons.
