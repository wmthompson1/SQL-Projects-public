Customer Order History Window

In the Customer Order History child window, customers are represented by an icon with a head on it. The Customer ID appears to the right of the icon.

VISUAL represents shipped orders with a gray order icon. Shipping Status: Shipped

VISUAL represents partially shipped orders with a yellow order icon. Shipping Status: Partially Shipped

VISUAL represents unshipped, on-time orders with a red order icon. Shipping Status: Not Shipped and Not Late

VISUAL represents unshipped, late orders, and orders currently on-hold, with a blue order icon. Shipping Status: Not Shipped and Late (Not Late for on-hold orders)

VISUAL represents cancelled/voided orders with a black order icon. Shipping Status: Cancellation Before Shipment

VISUAL represents order lines with blue screws. When you expand the Explorer tree by double-clicking any of the document icons or clicking a plus box, blue screws appear, one for each order line.
