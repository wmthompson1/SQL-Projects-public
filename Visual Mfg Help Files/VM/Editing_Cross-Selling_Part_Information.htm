Editing Cross-Selling Part Information

To edit cross-selling part information, call the appropriate part into the Part Maintenance window, click the Order Management tab and then click the Cross-selling Parts button. Double-click <Cross-selling Part ID> to view the part table and select a new Part ID.

However, take note that you cannot enter new Part IDs in the Cross-selling parts dialog box. To enter new parts, refer to the earlier section "Adding Part to the Database.

Refer to Editing Part Information for more on modifying parts in inventory.
