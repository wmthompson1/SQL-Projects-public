Account Assignments

The way you setup your database determines how your costing information is presented. Likewise, the way you assign your accounts in the database determines how the costs for a work order appear.

There are several places in which you can enter account information that affects the way costing information is reported. These areas are:

  • Shop Resource Maintenance

  • Service Maintenance

  • Part Maintenance

  • Employee Maintenance

  • Manufacturing Window

  • Customer Order Entry
