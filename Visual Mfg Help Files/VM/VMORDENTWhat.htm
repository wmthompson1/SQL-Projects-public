What is Customer Order Entry?

Customer Order Entry allows you to:

  • Enter and maintain customer orders for inventory and non-inventory parts.

  • Automatically create work orders to fulfill order line items. You can also link existing work orders to line items.

  • Automatically generate customer orders from quotations created in the Estimating Window.

  • Customer orders cause demand for parts from inventory or work from your plant floor.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, create customer orders on a site-by-site basis. You cannot combine multiple sites on the same order.
