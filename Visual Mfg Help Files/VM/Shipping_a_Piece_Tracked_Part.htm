Shipping a Piece Tracked Part

When you ship a customer order with piece tracked parts, specify the number of pieces to ship. The dimensions you specified on the customer order are inserted, but you can specify a different number of pieces and dimensions.

To ship piece tracked parts:

  1. Select Sales, Shipping Entry.

  2. Complete the header as you would for a standard part.

  3. The original number of pieces, the dimensions, and the calculated quantity from the customer order are inserted in the C.O. Pieces, C.O. Length, C.O. Height, C.O. Width, and Qty Ordered columns. Specify the actual pieces you are shipping to the customer by editing these fields:

Ship Pieces – Specify the number of pieces you are shipping to the customer. You must specify the number of pieces manually. You cannot use the Ship All feature with dimensional parts.

Length, Width, and Height – Specify the dimensions of the pieces you are shipping.

After you specify the number of pieces and the dimensions, the Ship Qty is calculated.

You can ship the pieces provided you have pieces in the dimensions you specified in your inventory.

  1. Complete the information on the Shipping window.

  2. Click Save.
