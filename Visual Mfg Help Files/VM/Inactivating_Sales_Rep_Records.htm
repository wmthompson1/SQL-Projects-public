Inactivating Sales Rep Records

If a sales representative is no longer associated with your company, you can inactivate the sales rep record. Users can not specify an inactive sales rep on customer orders, estimates, or accounts receivable invoices.

  1. Select Sales, Customer Maintenance.

  2. Select Maintain, Sales Reps.

  3. On the row that contains the sales rep record that you are inactivating, clear the Active check box.

  4. Click Save.
