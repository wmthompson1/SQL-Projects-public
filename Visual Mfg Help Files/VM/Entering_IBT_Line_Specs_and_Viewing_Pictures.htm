Entering IBT Line Specs and Viewing Pictures

  1. Click the Line Specs button. The Spec - Line note box appears.

  2. Enter the necessary information. After you save, the text appears in the Text column and VISUAL selects the Specs box, indicating that there is some kind of saved specification regarding the line item.

  3. With the appropriate line highlighted, click the Picture button to view a picture associated with the line.
