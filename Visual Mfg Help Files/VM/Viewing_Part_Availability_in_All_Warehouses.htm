Viewing Part Availability in All Warehouses

Expand the tree structure further by clicking the plus box next to the Part ID or by double-clicking the Part icon. The warehouses in which you currently stock the part appear. Highlight a warehouse to view part availability for that warehouse in the right pane.

Detail information for a part in a warehouse appears in the right pane as follows.

Warehouse ID - All warehouses that currently contain the focus part. Although you have established part/warehouse associations for this part, it does not mean that there currently is any available quantity of the part in the warehouse.

Available to Customer - The quantity of the part that is currently available to a customer. VISUAL determined this value based on the customer’s priority and disbursement fill rate, or the percent of the total available SKU quantity in a warehouse to which a customer has access.

Available - The quantity that is currently available to you to assign to a customer order. VISUAL determines this value by subtracting the Committed quantity of the part from the On Hand quantity of the part.

On Hand - The quantity of the part that you have in a warehouse or warehouse locations regardless of availability.

Committed - The portion of the On Hand quantity that is committed to customer orders, work order material requirements, and interbranch transfers.

Expected - The quantity of parts that you expect to receive from work orders, purchase orders, and interbranch transfers.

On Hold - The quantity of the part that you have placed on hold. These parts exist in inventory but are not available for order fulfillment. Place parts on hold in Warehouse Maintenance.

Locked - The quantity of the part that you have locked in inventory. These parts exist in inventory but are not available for order fulfillment. Lock parts in inventory in Warehouse Maintenance.

Unavailable - The quantity of the part that is unavailable for use in customer orders.

Minimum Inventory - The quantity of the part that you must keep in inventory.
