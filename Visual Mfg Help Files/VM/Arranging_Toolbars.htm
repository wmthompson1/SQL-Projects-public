Arranging Toolbars

You can arrange toolbars so they reside on any of the four sides of a VISUAL application or float independently atop the window. By default, toolbars are positioned at the top of the window. By placing your mouse pointer over the toolbar grippers you can drag the toolbar anywhere on the window. As you move the toolbar close to an area where it’s possible to dock the toolbar, the toolbar outline changes from a thick bold border to a normal style border. This is when you can release the grippers and dock the toolbar in the window.

Resizing Main Toolbars

When used as floating toolbars, you can resize toolbars by stretching them when the mouse pointer. Placed near a corner of toolbar, the mouse pointer becomes a resizer, vertical, horizontal, or diagonal, depending on where the pointer is. Move the resizer as necessary to stretch the toolbar. Resized any larger than the default size, the toolbar reverts to its default size when you dock it again in the window.
