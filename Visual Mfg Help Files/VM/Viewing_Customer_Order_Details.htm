Viewing Customer Order Details

  1. Select Sales, Order Management Window.

  2. If there is no order open in the Order Management Window, click the Options toolbar button, click the Customer tab, then click the Customer ID button to select a customer.

While in the Options dialog box, change the Dated Within and Dated Between settings if you want VISUAL to either widen or narrow its window of display. Choose dates well before and after the present date if you want to view a wide range of orders. To narrow the scope, enter Dated Between dates and click Apply.

Customer orders appear in the right pane.

  1. Double-click the customer icon.

The left explorer tree expands and customer orders appear.

The orders that appear depend on your date display settings.

Click the plus box to expand an order to see the number of lines it has.

  1. Highlight the order for which you want to view details.

The following columns appears in the right pane:

Order Line Number - The order line as it appears in relation to the other lines, if any, in the order.

Part ID - The Part ID of the order line.

Part Description - The description of the Part ID.

Order Date - The date on which the order was placed.

Part Status - The status of the order: A (active) or C (closed).

Order Quantity - The quantity of the part on the order line.

Shipped Quantity - The quantity to the current date that you have shipped to the customer

Last Shipped Date - The date on which you last shipped an order to the customer

Amount Ordered - The dollar amount of the order line.

Amount Shipped - The dollar amount of what you have shipped from the order line.

Shipping Status - The status of the order. Statuses include: Not Shipped & Late, Not Shipped & Not Late, Partially Shipped, Shipped, and Cancellation Before Shipment . Each status has a different colored icon.
