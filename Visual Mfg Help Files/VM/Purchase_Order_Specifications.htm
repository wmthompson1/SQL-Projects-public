Purchase Order Specifications

Specifications allow you to add free form text to a customer order. This text is printed on Purchase Order Acknowledgments, and can specify and document special needs, notes, and agreements for the customer order. You might use these specifications to specify packaging requirements, shipping details, configuration information, or other options that need to be specified along with a purchase order.

There are two types of customer order specifications (specs, for short): Order Specs and Line Specs. Order Specs are associated with the entire order, and Line Specs with individual line items.
