Viewing Committed Part Information for Work Order Material Requirements

Committed to Req part information is two levels down the Part Availability tree.

  1. In the Part Availability window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window. The structure expands to the next level.

  2. Highlight Committed and then click the plus box next to it. Double-click Committed to avoid clicking the plus box. The structure again expands.

  3. Highlight Committed RQ. Work Order material requirements for that part appear in the right pane. You can maximize the window and view the detail information in the right pane or click the Committed RQ plus box to expand the tree to the next level, where the detail information appears in both panes.

The Detail information appears in the right pane as follows:

Demand ID - The ID of the work order material requirement.

Date - The date on which the customer placed the order.

Quantity - The quantity of the material requirement.

Unallocated Demand - The quantity, if any, that you have yet to allocate to the material requirement.
