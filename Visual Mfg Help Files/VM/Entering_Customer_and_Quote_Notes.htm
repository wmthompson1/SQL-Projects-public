Entering Customer and Quote Notes

Customer Notes and Quote Notes are basically the same. The difference between them is that Customer Notes are tied to the customer and Quote Notes are tied to the quote. That is, a note you enter for a customer in one quote also appears in every other quote that the customer receives. The note you enter for a quote only appears in the quote in which they were written.

  1. With the appropriate quote open in the Estimating Window, click the Quote Notes or Cust Notes button.

The Notations dialog box appears.

  1. Click the New button to make a new entry.

A line appears with the current date and time for the entry.

  1. Enter the appropriate information.

You can enter an unlimited amount of information.

Use the Paste button to paste text into the dialog box that you copied from another location.

Use the Copy button to copy text in this dialog box to the clipboard.

  1. Click the Save button to save the Notes.

  2. Click Close button to close the Notations dialog box and return to the Estimating Window.
