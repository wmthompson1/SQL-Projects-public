What is Inventory Transaction Entry?

Use Inventory Transaction Entry to perform these functions:

  • Issuing and returning inventory materials to work orders. Materials purchased directly to work orders are issued by Purchase Receipt Entry, and cannot be issued from here. Use the main Inventory Transaction Entry window to issue materials one-by-one. To issue all materials required by a work order, use the Issue by Exception function.

  • Receiving and returning finished goods from work in process. Work orders fully linked to a customer order are received to inventory and issued to the customer order automatically by Shipping Entry, and cannot be received from here. You can receive finished goods by work order or by part.

  • Adjusting inventory on-hand quantities to reconcile with actual physical quantities. Physical Inventory Count does this automatically.

  • Viewing of inventory transactions created automatically by Purchase Receipt Entry, Shipping Entry, Physical Inventory Count, and Labor Ticket Entry auto-issues.

  • Transferring inventory between part locations
