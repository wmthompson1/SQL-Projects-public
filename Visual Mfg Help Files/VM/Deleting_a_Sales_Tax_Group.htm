Deleting a Sales Tax Group

You can delete tax groups from the database, but only if no customer orders are associated with the tax group you are deleting.

Deleting a Sales Tax Group permanently removes the information from the database. You can NOT recover deleted tax group information.

To delete a tax group from the database:

  1. Call the tax group you want to delete to the Tax Group Maintenance window and click the Delete button.

A dialog box appears, asking you to confirm the deletion.

  1. Click Yes to complete the deletion. Click No to cancel.

The Sales Tax Group Maintenance window still contains the tax group information, but the tax group is no longer in the database.

  1. Click the Clear button to complete the operation.
