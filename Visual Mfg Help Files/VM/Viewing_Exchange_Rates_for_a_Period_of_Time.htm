Viewing Exchange Rates for a Period of Time

You can view what the exchange rate was for a currency for a period of time by manually entering a date or clicking on the calendar icon to select a date and then clicking Lookup Rates.

In the line item table, VISUAL displays what the exchange rate was for that currency during the time you specified.

You can use this value or reselect the current date to use up-to-date exchange rates for the current purchase order.
