Deleting Service Information in a Single-site Environment

Deleting a service permanently removes the information from the database; you cannot recover a deleted service. You cannot delete a service if it is being used elsewhere in the system.

  1. Call the service you want to delete into the Service Maintenance window.

  2. Click the Delete button.

A dialog box appears, asking you to confirm the deletion.

  1. Click Yes to continue or No to cancel the deletion.

The Service Maintenance window still contains the service information, but the service has been removed from the database.

  1. Click the Clear button.
