Deleting Existing Customers

Deleting a customer will permanently remove the information from the database, and is not recoverable. If another application references this customer, an error message appears informing you that you can not delete the customer. The most common references are existing customer orders, shipments and Accounts Receivable invoices.

  1. Call the customer you want to delete into the Customer Maintenance window.

  2. Click the Delete button.

A dialog box appears, asking you to confirm the deletion.

  1. Click Yes to continue, or No to cancel the deletion.

VISUAL removes the customer from the database, though the Customer Maintenance window still contains the customer information.

If the customer is linked to other records in your database, VISUAL does not allow you to delete the customer.

  1. Click the Clear button to clear the customer information from the window.
