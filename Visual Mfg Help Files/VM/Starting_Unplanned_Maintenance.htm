Starting Unplanned Maintenance

Unplanned Maintenance is available from the VISUAL Equipment Maintenance menu.

From the Infor VISUAL main menu, select Unplanned Maintenance from the Equipment Maintenance menu.

The Unplanned Maintenance window appears.

The Unplanned Maintenance window is divided into a header section and a tabbed section. Do not concern yourself with entering data in any of the fields just yet. Begin by entering general unplanned maintenance information. See the next section for more information.
