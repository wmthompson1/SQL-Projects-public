What is Project Billing Entry?

Because companies in the Project and Aerospace and Defense industries invoice their customers for milestones and other non-shipping items, you must perform your Project Billing using the Project Billing Entry window.

Project Billing is a three-step process:

  • Create Billing Events for your Customer Order lines.

  • Mark each of the appropriate Billing Events for invoice generation.

  • Print your Invoices automatically transferring them to Account Receivable.
