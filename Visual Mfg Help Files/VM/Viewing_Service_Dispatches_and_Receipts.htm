Viewing Service Dispatches and Receipts

You can view records of all outside services that you dispatch via a linked purchase order from the Manufacturing Window in the Work Order Service Dispatch and Receipts line item table.

To view service dispatches and receipts:

  1. Select Eng/Mfg, Manufacturing Window.

  2. Open the master whose costs you want to view.

  3. Select Info, Service Dispatches and Receipts.
