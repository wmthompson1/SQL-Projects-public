What is the Purchase Management Window?

Efficient procurement requires that you know exactly what your manufacturing and sales demands are, and will be, and purchase the supply necessary to fulfill that demand. Depending on your company’s requirements, will depend on how much supply you procure and how far ahead from a purchasing standpoint that you plan. Using the Purchase Management Window, you can easily view your demand history and purchase accordingly.

Instead of printing a Material Planning Report, you can now use the Purchase Management Window to view your demand. By using the refresh buttons in the viewing child windows, you can view up to date information as other company members update your demand.

The Purchase Management Window includes a powerful search dialog, enabling you to customize the information appearing in your child windows and focus on the information you need for the products on which you are working. For example, in one child window you can have a list of parts and their requirements sorted by preferred vendor. In another grid you can have purchasing history for the part sorted by vendor. You can then drag the demand for a particular part to a vendor and have it create a new purchase order for that combination.
