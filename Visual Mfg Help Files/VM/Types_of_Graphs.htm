Types of Graphs

When you can choose the type of graph, a Graph Type field is provided on the graph dialog. Not all types of graphs are available in all graph dialogs, and some graph dialogs do not provide you the opportunity to select a graph.

These graph types are available:

  • Area

  • Area Step

  • Bar

  • Bar Horizontal

  • Doughnut

  • Line

  • Line Step

  • Pie

  • Spline

  • Spline Area
