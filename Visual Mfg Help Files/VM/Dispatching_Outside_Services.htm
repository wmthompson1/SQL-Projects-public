Dispatching Outside Services

You can dispatch services on the fly in Purchase Order Entry by entering the quantity you want to dispatch in the Dispatch Quantity column in the line item table and selecting the appropriate Ship to Address.

  1. Click the Ship To Addr button in the Dispatch tab.

The Ship To/Purchase Addresses dialog box appears.

  1. Select a Vendor Address and then click Use Selected Address.

The address that you select appears in the Ship To/Ship From field in the main Purchase Order Entry window.

However, after you enter all other necessary information for the purchase order, and click Save, the original Vendor ID and Address reappears in the Ship To/Ship From field, and the quantity that you entered in the Dispatch Quantity column now appears in the Total Quantity Dispatched column. You can view any record of this dispatch in the Service Dispatch Entry window.
