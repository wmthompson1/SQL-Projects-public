Deleting Employee Information

Deleting an employee will permanently remove the information from the database. Deleted employee information is not recoverable. However, you will not be allowed to delete an employee that has associated information.

  1. Call the employee you want to delete into the Employee Maintenance window.

  2. Click the Delete button.

A dialog box appears, asking you to confirm the deletion.

  1. Click Yes to continue, or No to cancel the deletion.

If you click Yes, VISUAL removes the employee information from the database, though the Employee Maintenance window still contains the information.

  1. Click the Clear button to clear the employee information from the window.
