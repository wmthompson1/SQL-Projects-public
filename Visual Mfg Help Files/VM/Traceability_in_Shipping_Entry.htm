Traceability in Shipping Entry

Use Traceability in Shipping Entry to record the lot numbers or serial numbers of shipped parts. You may be beginning traceability with shipment. It is required when the Issuing/Shipping option is checked in When to Apply section for the part’s traceability profile. Traceable parts are indicated by a check mark in the Trc column of the line item table.
