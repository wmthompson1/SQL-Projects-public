Enabling Customer Order Consolidation

Consolidation is the process of combining license-plated (or otherwise uniquely identified) inventory into a single container. Partial pallets may be consolidated and broken cases may be consolidated. Generally, this means combining of things that have common characteristics, such as pallets of the same product.

With the appropriate Customer ID in the Customer ID field, click the Order Management tab.

Select the Consolidate Orders box if you want to allow order consolidation.
