Viewing General Part Information

The Part Availability child window displays part availability one part at a time. If the Order Entry child window is open with an order loaded, the Part Availability child window shows part information for any part in the order, depending on which order line you highlight. Without the Order Entry child window open, click the Options button on the Main toolbar, click the Part tab, and then select a part to view.

To view more availability details, double-click the Part Info icon, double click the Part Availability icon, then highlight the part that appears. The warehouses in which you currently stock the part appear in the right pane.
