Evaluating Customer Credit

You can view information about a customer’s credit-worthiness directly in Customer Order Entry. The system uses information you defined in the customer’s record in Customer Maintenance to help you identify potential credit risks. Use the Credit tab to view basic information about the customer’s credit performance. Use the toolbar buttons to view the customer’s credit and payment ratings.

If you are licensed to use multiple entities, credit information is available on an entity-by-entity basis. If you are licensed use a single entity, credit information is available on an enterprise-wide basis.
