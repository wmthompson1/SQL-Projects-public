Using Part Trace Maintenance

When you are specifically interested in viewing, reporting on, and managing traceability information for one or more parts, use the Part Trace Maintenance application.

Part Trace Maintenance provides the following functions:

  • Full viewing and editing of all trace records in existence for a traceable part.

  • Viewing of related Inventory and Labor transactions associated with a lot number.

  • Pre-Assigning of lot numbers and quantities for a part.
