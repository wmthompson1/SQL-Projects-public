The Production Schedule

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, each site has one production schedule. The Manufacturing and Scheduling Window assumes that this schedule is the “actual” plan for the site. Dates from the production schedule are the ones shown on the manufacturing window display for scheduled work orders. They are also displayed in the Scheduling Window. If a work order or operation is not scheduled, it is marked Not Sched.

If you are licensed to use a single site, you have one production schedule for your entire enterprise.
