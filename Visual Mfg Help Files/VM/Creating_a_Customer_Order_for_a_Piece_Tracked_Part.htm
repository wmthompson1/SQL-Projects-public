Creating a Customer Order for a Piece Tracked Part

To place a customer order for a piece tracked part:

  1. From the main menu, select Sales, Customer Order Entry.

  2. Complete the upper part of the sales order as you would for a standard part.

  3. Click the Insert Row button.

  4. Double-click the Part ID browse button and select the piece tracked part your customer is ordering.

  5. Enter the number of pieces your customer is ordering in the # Pieces column.

  6. According to the dimensional requirements for this material (set up on the Piece Trk tab of the Part Maintenance window), enter the required dimensions for the Length, Width, and Height for this part.

When you move the cursor to a different location, the Quantity is calculated based on requirements for this part.

  1. Click the Save button.

Editing the Delivery Schedule

You can create delivery schedules for orders with piece tracked parts. Instead of specifying a quantity to ship in the Delivery Schedule dialog box, specify the number of pieces to ship.
