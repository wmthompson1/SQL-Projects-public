Deleting A Customer Order

You can remove the customer order from the system, including all of its line items.

Deleting a customer order permanently removes the order and its line items from the database. You cannot recover the order after you delete it.

  1. If you are licensed to use multiple sites, click the Site ID arrow and select the site that contains the order. If you are licensed to use a single site, this field is unavailable.

  2. Call the customer order you want to delete into the Customer Order Entry window,

  3. Click the Delete button.

  4. You cannot delete a Customer Order if it has any shipments against it.

  5. A dialog box appears, asking you to confirm the deletion.

  6. Click Yes to permanently remove the customer order, or No to leave the order in the system.

  7. Click the Clear button to complete the operation.

  8. VISUAL does not delete a linked work order when you delete or cancel a customer order. It is up to you to appropriately manage the remaining order, which you may not want to delete.

    Use the Cancel status when you want a permanent record of the Customer Order. You can always re-release a canceled order later, if needed.
