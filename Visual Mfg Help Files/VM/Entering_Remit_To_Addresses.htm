Entering Remit To Addresses

If a vendor uses remit to addresses that are different from their mailing address, specify them using this procedure. A vendor can have as many different remit to addresses as necessary. These steps describe the procedure for adding remit to addresses.

  1. Select Maintain, Remit To Addresses .

  1. Click the Insert button.

  1. Specify the information for the Remit To Address in the address fields.

The information automatically appears in the table as you tab through the address fields. You can also copy and paste information from one line to another.

Deleting Remit To Addresses

To remove a Remit To address completely, highlight the address line and click Delete.
