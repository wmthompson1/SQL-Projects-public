Entering, Maintaining, and Deleting Coverage Times

Use the Coverage Time Maintenance dialog box to enter and maintain coverage times information.

  1. From the Maintenance menu, select Coverage Hours Maintenance.

The Coverage Hours Maintenance dialog box appears.

  1. To enter a new range, click the Insert button.

  2. Enter information in the following fields:

Start Time - The start time of the maintenance coverage shift. VISUAL assigns AM unless you specify PM.

End Time - The end time of the maintenance coverage shift. VISUAL assigns AM unless you specify PM.

Coverage Description - A description of the coverage type. Be detailed in your descriptions. For example, enter text that helps to clarify what a maintenance engineer may do during this type of maintenance coverage shift.

  1. Click the Save button to commit the information.

  2. To modify information, place the mouse pointer in the appropriate column in the appropriate line and manually edit the information. When finished, click the Save button.

  3. To delete a maintenance coverage range, highlight the appropriate line and click the Delete button.

VISUAL places an X at the beginning of the line.

Click the Save button to complete the deletion.

  1. Click the Close button to return to the Equipment Maintenance window.
