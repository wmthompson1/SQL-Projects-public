Entering and Maintaining Equipment Information

Before using the Equipment Maintenance window, take a little time to enter some basic information regarding equipment and services.

From the Maintenance menu, you can enter and maintain equipment information of the following types:

  • Equipment Type

  • Equipment Model

  • Service Area Maintenance

  • Service Branch Maintenance

  • Maintenance Type

  • Coverage Hours Maintenance

  • Part Price List Maintenance

  • Labor Price List Maintenance

  • Labor Calendar Maintenance

  • Auto Numbering
