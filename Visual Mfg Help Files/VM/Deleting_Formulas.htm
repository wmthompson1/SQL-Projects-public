Deleting Formulas

If you no longer use certain formulas within VISUAL Price Book, you can delete them from your database.

To delete a Formula from VISUAL Price Book:

  1. Open the record you want to remove from the VISUAL Price Book database.

  2. On the VISUAL Price Book main toolbar, click the Delete button to remove the formula from the database. VISUAL prompts you to confirm the deletion.

  3. Click Yes to remove the record from the database. Click No to cancel the deletion.
