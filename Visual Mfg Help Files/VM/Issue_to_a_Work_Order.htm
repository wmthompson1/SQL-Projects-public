Issue to a Work Order

Materials issued to Work Orders are a key element in relieving inventory. The VISUAL BTS system will allow instantaneous material positions in both inventory and work-in-process. This constant accurate material issues to workorders provide up-to-date information at all times.

The chain of events is similar to the following:

  • WorkOrder Pick List is printed

  • Material is Located (and location is scanned)

  • Material is moved to a production area

  • Material is scanned to relieve Inventory and applied to Work Order
