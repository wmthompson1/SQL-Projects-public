Removing an Analytic

You can permanently remove an analytic from the dashboard, or you can temporarily remove an analytic.

To remove an analytic:

  1. Select File, Dashboard.

  2. In the dashboard, select File, Open and select the dashboard to use.

  3. Click the Close button in the upper right corner of the analytic.

  4. To permanently remove the analytic from the dashboard, click Save.

  5. To temporarily remove the analytic, do not click Save. To redisplay the analytic, click Restore.
