Deleting Test Step Definitions

You can delete test step definitions in Test Definition.

  1. Highlight the test step line item that you want to delete.

  2. Click the line item Delete button.

A dialog box appears, asking you to confirm the deletion.

  1. Click Yes to confirm the deletion.

The test step line item disappears from the table.

  1. Click the Save button to complete the deletion.
