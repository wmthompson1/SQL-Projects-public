Activities in Outside Service Maintenance

Activities are available in Outside Service Maintenance. Use activities to manually create reminders about follow-up items or to record information about services.

To add a new activity, select Edit, Activity Entry. See Creating Activities.

To view existing activities, select View, Activities. To view all activities related to all outside services, select View, Activities from a blank Outside Service Maintenance window. To view all activities related to a particular outside service, select the outside service in Outside Service Maintenance before selecting View, Activities. See Reviewing Activities.
