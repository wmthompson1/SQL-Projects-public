Enabling Customer Compliance Labeling

Compliance Labeling is the process of labeling outbound cartons or pallets in the warehouse (moveable units) with a bar-coded label specific to a customer requirement. In the warehouse, there may be many differing label requirements.

With the appropriate Customer ID in the Customer ID field, click the Order Management tab.

Select the Compliance Labeling box if you require that the ship-to address have compliance labeling.
