What is Vendor Inquiry?

The Vendor Inquiry window provides an easy and versatile way to view different types of database information about your purchase orders. You can view Purchase Order IDs, Vendor Part IDs, Invoice IDs, Invoice Account IDs and Receiver IDs.

There are several tools in Vendor Inquiry to make the inquiry easier to use and more specific. As with other search tools in VISUAL, you can perform searches using a wide variety of options.
