## How to Document a Decision

1. **Create a new file** using the naming pattern: `YYYY-MM-DD_decision-topic.md`
2. **Use the template** from [Templates/decision-template.md](../Templates/decision-template.md)
3. **Link it here** in the decision log table above

## Types of Decisions We Track

- **Architecture & Design** - System design choices, technology selections
- **Process Changes** - Workflow modifications, new procedures
- **Policy** - Business rules, standards, governance
- **Data** - Data models, naming conventions, quality standards
- **Tool Selection** - Software/tool choices

## Decision Review

Decisions are reviewed quarterly to ensure they remain valid and are being followed.
