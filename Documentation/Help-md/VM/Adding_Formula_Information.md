# Adding Formula Information

Enter the formula you want to use on the Formula tab.
Visual Basic is used as the formula language. Although the intention
of this document is not to teach you Visual Basic, it should serve
as a guide to how you may create formulas.

## To add Formula tab information:

1. On the Formulas window,
   click the Formula tab. Formula Information
   appears in the field. If this is a new formula, the Formula tab
   is empty.
2. In the field, enter
   the formula you want to use, based on the information you enter
   on the Formulas window. For more information on the formulas used
   within VISUAL Price Book, refer to Appendix #.
3. On the VISUAL Price
   Book main toolbar, click the Save button
   to save your changes to the Formulas window.
