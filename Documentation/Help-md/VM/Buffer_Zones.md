# Buffer Zones

Buffer zones indicate that a certain percentage of the buffer has
been consumed. You can use this information to analyze whether your
buffers are too small or too large.

These zones are used: Green, Yellow, and Red. For time buffers,
the Black zone is also used. The Black zone indicates that all of
the red zone has been consumed. When you enter the Black zone, you
have used all of the buffer time.
