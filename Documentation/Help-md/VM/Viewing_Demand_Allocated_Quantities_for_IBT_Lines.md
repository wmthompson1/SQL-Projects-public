# Viewing Demand Allocated Quantities for IBT Lines

After you allocate supply from an IBT line to demand,
you can view allocation information for the IBT line in the line item
table.

The following two columns display important allocation information:

Supply Quantity Allocated - The quantity
of parts that you have allocated to a demand line. This quantity can
never be greater that the quantity of the line.

The quantity in this column less the order quantity of the line
is the quantity available to you to allocate to demand.

Demand Quantity Fulfilled - The quantity
of parts that the supplying source has fulfilled.
