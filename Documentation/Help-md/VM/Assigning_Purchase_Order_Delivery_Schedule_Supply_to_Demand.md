# Assigning Purchase Order Delivery Schedule Supply to Demand

Use the same procedures to assign purchase order delivery
schedule line supply to demand as you use to assign purchase order
line supply to demand.

* You can assign to these
  five demand sources from a purchase order delivery schedule line:
* Customer Order Delivery
  Schedule supply
* Customer Order supply
* Interbranch Transfer supply
* Work Order supply
* Inventory (Warehouse) supply
