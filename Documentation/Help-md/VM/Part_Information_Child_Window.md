## Part Information Child Window

In the Part Information child window, parts in general are represented
by a screw icon. This icon represents the top level of the part information
Explorer tree.

In the Part Information child window, available parts are represented
by a variation of the screw icon. This icon represents the top level of
the part availability portion of the Part Information Explorer tree.

In the Part Information child window, substitute parts are represented
by a double-screw icon.

In the Part Information child window, cross-selling parts are also represented
by a variation on the part information screw icon.

In the Part Information child window, a factory/warehouse icon represents
warehouses.

If more than one warehouse exists for a part, every other warehouse
is represented by the same icon of a different color.

In the Part Information child window, VISUAL represents committed orders
containing the focus part with a fluorescent green
order icon. Committed orders are customer orders.

In the Part Information child window, VISUAL represents committed orders
containing the focus part with supply coming some time in the future (Order
Status: Future) with a fluorescent green
order icon minus internal color.

In the Part Information child window, VISUAL represents expected orders
containing the focus part with a dark blue
order icon. Expected orders are purchase orders and work orders.
