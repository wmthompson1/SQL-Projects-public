# Step Five

## Re-run Costing Utilities and Post Manufacturing Journals

Repeat steps three and four until the Costing Utilities
indicate that no updates have been performed or when the number of
updates remains constant (penny rounding sometimes moves back and
forth between transactions, which results in costing reporting an
update to a transaction).
