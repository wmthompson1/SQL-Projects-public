# Netting Co-products

The [Material Planning Window](VMPLNWINWhat.htm)
provides a view of a parts supply and demand. This is called netting.
This procedure must handle co-products. A co-product implies that
the work order produces each of the parts in the co-product list,
therefore, netting must "see" these quantities of parts
in its analysis of a parts supply picture.
