# Assigning IBT Receipt Quantities to Demand

As you receive IBT lines into the appropriate receiving
warehouse location, you can also at that time choose to assign all
or a portion of this received quantity to sources of demand. Sources
of demand include, customer orders, customer order delivery schedules,
inventory, work order material requirement demand, work order material
operation demand, and interbranch transfers.

You can assign IBT receipt quantities to the following sources of
demand:

Order Delivery Schedules](Assigning_IBT_Line_Supply_to_Customer_Order_Delivery_Schedule_Demand.htm)

Orders](Assigning_IBT_Line_Supply_to_Customer_Order_Demand.htm)
