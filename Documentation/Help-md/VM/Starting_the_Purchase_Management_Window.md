# Starting the Purchase Management Window

To start the Purchase Management Window:

From VISUALs Purchasing menu, select the Purchase
Management Window option.

The Purchase Management Window appears.

The Purchase Order Entry window appears as a child window within
the Purchase Management Window. Notice that the Purchase Order Entry
toolbar appears in the Purchase Management toolbar and not the Purchase
Order Entry child window.
