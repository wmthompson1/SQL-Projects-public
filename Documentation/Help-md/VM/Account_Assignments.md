# Account Assignments

The way you setup your database determines how your
costing information is presented. Likewise, the way you assign your
accounts in the database determines how the costs for a work order
appear.

There are several places in which you can enter account information
that affects the way costing information is reported. These areas
are:

* [Shop
  Resource Maintenance](Shop_Resource_Maintenance.md)
* [Service
  Maintenance](Service_Maintenance.md)
* [Part
  Maintenance](Part_Maintenance.md)
* [Employee
  Maintenance](VMEMPMNTfrmEmployee.md)
* [Manufacturing
  Window](VMMFGWINWhat.md)
* [Customer
  Order Entry](VMORDENTWhat.md)
