# Maintaining Purchase Price Information

Use these options available on the Maintain menu to specify the
vendors that sell this part to you:

* [Vendors
  Supplying This Part](Establishing_Preferred_Vendors.md)
* [Vendor
  Parts Supplied](Adding_Vendor_Quotes.md)
* [Purchase
  Contract](Viewing_Purchase_Contracts_Part.md)

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, you must define Vendors
Supplying This Part and Vendor Parts Supplied at the Site level. You
can view Purchase Contracts at either the tenant level or the site
level.
