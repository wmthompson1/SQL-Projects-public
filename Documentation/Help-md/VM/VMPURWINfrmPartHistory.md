# Using the Part History Tool

Use the Part History child window to view the usage
history of your parts. VISUAL populates the Part History child window
with history for the part you select. The Part History child window
populates only from your selection in the Part Demand child window.

The Part History tool has three levels of information: Part, Demand
History, and Transactions.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, the Part Demand tool
shows information for all of your sites.

## Viewing Part Usage

If you want to view a parts usage by period, click
the part in which you are interested in the explorer panel.

Each financial period appears with the total quantity of the part
you used within that period.

## Viewing Part Demand History

To view demand history for a part:

If the demand for the part comes from a project, that project information
also appears.

## Viewing Part Transaction History

To view the transaction history for period, expand
the part in the explorer by clicking the plus icon and click the period
in which you are interested.

## Viewing Part History Graphs

At the demand history level, you can view information
as a graph by clicking the Graph button.

To return back to the table view, click the Table
button.
