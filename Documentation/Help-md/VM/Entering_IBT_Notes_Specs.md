# Entering IBT Notes & Specs

An easy and quick reference, IBT Notes boxes allow
you to enter and save information regarding an IBT.

1. Click the IBT Notes button located underneath
   and to the right of the line item table. The IBT Notes dialog
   box appears.
2. Click the New button.
3. Enter necessary text
   or notes and click Save.

Follow this same procedure for entering
specs.
