# Viewing WIP/VAS Information for a Part in the Customer Order Line Level

After you assign an eligible part a WIP/VAS value,
you can view the parts WIP/VAS unit price on the C/O line.

After you save an order, VISUAL fills the WIP/VAS Unit Price column
in the line item table with the amount per unit that you charge for
WIP/VAS.
