# VISUAL Price Book Within VISUAL Order Management

VISUAL Price Book works as a standalone application
outside of Infor VISUAL, but its current design works seamlessly with
the Infor VISUAL Order Management Window. After debugging your formulas
for Parts and Prices, you can view their results within VE.
