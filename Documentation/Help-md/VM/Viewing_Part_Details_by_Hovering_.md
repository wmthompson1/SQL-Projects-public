# Viewing Part Details by Hovering

The Order Management Window is geared towards accepting
and processing an exceptionally high volume of customer orders. Many
orders may contain multiple lines, and some of those lines may be
for parts of which you have no firsthand knowledge. When entering
complex, multi-line customer orders, it would be helpful to know if
a part has any cross-selling or substitute parts defined for it, or
if WIP/VAS is available for the part. Rather than opening Part Maintenance
and calling up the appropriate part master to check on this information,
rely on the Order Management Windows unique part information pop-ups.
To view pop-ups, place your mouse pointer, or hover, over the Part
ID field.
