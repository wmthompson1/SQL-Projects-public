# Maintaining ECN Tasks

After VISUAL has generated tasks for your ECNs, whether
simultaneously or individually, the users you have assigned as team
members of an ECN, must appropriately change the statuses of those
tasks. Use the ECN Task Maintenance window to manage and maintain
your ECN tasks.

See [What is Task Maintenance](VMTSKMNTWhat.htm).
