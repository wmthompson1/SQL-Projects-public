# Viewing Customer Order Delivery Schedule Line Allocation Information

The quantity of supply that you have allocated to
a customer order delivery schedule line appears in the Allocated Quantity
column in the Edit Delivery Schedule dialog box line item table.

With the appropriate customer order open, highlight the delivery
schedule line for which you want to view allocation information and
click the Del Sched tab. The Edit Delivery
Schedule dialog box appears.

The quantity of supply that you have allocated to this customer
order delivery schedule line appears in the Quantity Allocated column.

Until the quantity equals the quantity of the customer order delivery
schedule line, you can still allocate supply to the delivery schedule
line from one of the six available supplying sources.
