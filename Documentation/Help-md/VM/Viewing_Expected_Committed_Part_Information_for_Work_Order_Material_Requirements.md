# Viewing Expected/Committed Part Information for Work Order Material Requirements

Expected/Committed to Req part information is two
levels down the Part Availability tree.

1. In the Part Availability
   window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window.
   The structure expands to the next level.
2. Highlight Expected/Committed
   and then click the plus box next to it. Double-click Committed
   to avoid clicking the plus box. The structure again expands.
3. Highlight Expected/Committed
   RQ.

Work Order material requirements for that
part appear in the right pane. You can maximize the window and view
the detail information in the right pane or click the Committed RQ
plus box to expand the tree to the next level, where the detail information
appears in both panes.

The Detail information appears in the right
pane as follows:

Demand ID - The
ID of the work order material requirement.

Date - The date
on which you began the work order.

Quantity - The
quantity of the material requirement.

Unallocated Demand
- The quantity, if any, that you have yet to allocate to the material
requirement.
