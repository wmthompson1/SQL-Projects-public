# Consumable Parts

Consumable parts have Part IDs even though you cannot
receive them into inventory. VISUAL expenses consumables upon receipt,
so a consumable materials quantity at any one time is not important.
You can, however, assign consumable parts preferred vendors so they
can accumulate a purchase history.
