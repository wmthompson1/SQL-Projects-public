# Viewing Expected Part Information for Work Orders

You can view information regarding parts that you
expect through work order production two levels down on the Part Availability
tree.

1. In the Part Availability
   window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window.
   The structure expands to the next level.
2. Highlight Expected
   and then click the plus box next to it. Double-click Expected
   to avoid clicking the plus box. The structure again expands.
3. Highlight Expected
   WO. Work orders for that part appear in the right pane.
   You can maximize the window and view the detail information in
   the right pane or click the Expected WO
   plus box to expand the tree to the next level, where the detail
   information appears in both panes.

The Detail information that appears in the
right pane as follows:

Supply ID - The
ID of the work order.

Date - The date
on which you started the work order.

Order Quantity
- The quantity of the work order.

Received - The
quantity of the work order that you have received as finished product
into inventory.

Allocated - The
portion of the work order quantity that you have allocated to demand.

Unallocated Demand
- The quantity, if any, that you have yet to allocate from the work
order to demand.
