# Allocating Supply to Customer Orders

With the customer orders order line to which you
wan to allocate supply highlighted, from the Edit menu select Allocations and then Allocate
Supply to Customer Order. The Supply Links dialog box appears.

You can allocate supply to a customer order from these sources:

* Co-product supply
* Inventory
* Purchase Order Delivery
  Schedule supply
* Purchase Order supply
* Interbranch Transfer supply
* Work Order Supply
