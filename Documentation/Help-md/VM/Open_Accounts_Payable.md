# Open Accounts Payable

This [dashboard](VMDBOARDWhat.htm) analytic
shows the monetary value of outstanding accounts payable invoices.

Type: Bar Graph

Y-axis: Amount (expressed in the accounting
entitys functional currency)

Series categories (bars): Overall, Vendor

Series groupings (X-axis): Day, Week,
Month, Quarter, Year

Date filter applies to: Invoice Date

Filters: Vendor ID, Site ID

Detail grid: Click a section of the bar
to view the invoices that contribute to the bar.
