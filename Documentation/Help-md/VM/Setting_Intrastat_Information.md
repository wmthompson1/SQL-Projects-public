# Setting Intrastat Information

Intrastat is a European Union term that refers to
the system of tracking statistics of goods manufactured and distributed
to and from EC member countries. Intrastat is intended to ease the
burden to manufacturers and distributors of goods who import and export
within the EC by eliminating customs declarations.

Intrastat requires that goods sold and manufactured in the EC be
tracked so that their origin and destination is recorded in some permanent
way. Periodically, each manufacturer and distributor of goods must
report those items sold and distributed, where they were manufactured
or obtained, and where they were sent, including intermediate distribution
points (when the origin or destination is outside of the country where
the manufacturer or distributor resides).

Intrastat applies to the importation and exportation of goods. It
does not apply to goods shipped within a given member country. Only
VAT registered companies are required to provide Intrastat information.

The VISUAL Intrastat feature provides the appropriate information
to be placed on required reports being made to the governing country
in which the manufacturer operates and records the supporting data
on which the reports are based. The exact form and format of these
reports varies from country to country. You may need edit these reports
to suit your individual needs.

Click the Intrastat tab to view the Intrastat section of the Accounting
Entity Maintenance window.
