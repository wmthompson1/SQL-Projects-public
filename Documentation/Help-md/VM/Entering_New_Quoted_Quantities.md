# Entering New Quoted Quantities

You can enter new quoted quantities in the Quote Price
dialog box.

1. Click the Insert
   button.

A new line appears in the Quantity line
item table.

2. Enter the information
   for the new quantity.
3. Click the Save
   button to save the new information.
