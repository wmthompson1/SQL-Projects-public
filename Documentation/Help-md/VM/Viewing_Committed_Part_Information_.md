# Viewing Committed Part Information for Interbranch Transfers

Committed to IBT part information is two levels down
the Part Availability tree.

1. In the Part Availability
   window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window.
   The structure expands to the next level.
2. Highlight Committed
   and then click the plus box next to it. Double-click Committed
   to avoid clicking the plus box. The structure again expands.
3. Highlight Committed
   IBT. IBTs for that part appear in the right pane. You can
   maximize the window to better view the detail information in the
   right pane or click the Committed IBT plus box to expand the tree
   to the next level, where the detail information appears in both
   panes.

The Detail information appears in the right
pane as follows:

Demand ID - The
ID of the IBT.

Date - The date
on which you initiated the transfer.

Quantity - The
quantity of the IBT.

Unallocated Demand
- The quantity, if any, that you have yet to allocate to the IBT.
