# Assigning Interbranch Supply to Demand

As you can assign supply to outgoing IBT lines, you
can in turn assign outgoing IBTs to demand.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, you can assign a quantity
to demand for transactions entered in the To Warehouse IDs site.
For example, if the warehouse you specified in the to warehouse site
belongs to site MMC-SITE B, then only transactions created in MMC-SITE
B are eligible for selection in the Assign to Demand dialog box.

to demand:
