# Viewing the Engineering Master for a Fabricated Part

If a material in a master is a fabricated part, you
can open the parts default engineering master. You can access this
feature from the Header Card, from a Leg header card, and from the
Material card.

If you have enabled the pop up menu, you can right-click the Material
card and select Open Engineering Master
to open the master for the requirement.

To open a master from an open Material card or Leg/Detail card,
select Info, Open Engineering
Master.

To open a master from an open Header card, click the Engineering
Master button.
