# Removing an Individual Tax from a Tax Group

You may need to remove an individual tax from a tax
group.

To do this:

1. Select the individual
   tax from the table of tax IDs.
2. Click the Del
   Row button.

An x appears in the left-most column of
the table.

3. Click Save
   to save your changes.

VISUAL removes the individual tax from the
tax group.
