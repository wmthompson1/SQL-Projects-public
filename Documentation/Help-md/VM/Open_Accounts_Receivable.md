# Open Accounts Receivable

This [dashboard](VMDBOARDWhat.md) analytic
shows the monetary value of outstanding accounts receivable invoices.

Type: Bar Graph

Y-axis: Amount (expressed in the accounting
entitys functional currency)

Series categories (bars): Overall, Customer,
Sales Rep

Series groupings (X-axis): Day, Week,
Month, Quarter, Year

Date filter applies to: Invoice Date

Filters: Customer ID, Sales Rep ID, Site
ID

Detail grid: Click a bar to open the [Open Accounts Receivable
Detail](Open_Accounts_Receivable_Detail.md) grid filtered to display the invoices that contribute to
the bar.
