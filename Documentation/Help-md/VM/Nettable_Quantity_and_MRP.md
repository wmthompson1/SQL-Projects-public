# Nettable Quantity and MRP

These quantities are included in the nettable quantity:

* Quantities in Regular locations
  whose status is Available or On Hold
* Quantities in Floor Stock
  locations whose status is Available or On Hold

Quantities in Transit locations are not included in the nettable
quantity regardless of the status. Quantities in locations with the
status Unavailable are also not included in the nettable quantity.
