# What is Accounts Payable?

Accounts Payable (A/P) records and manages amounts owed to vendors for goods and services received. It includes invoice entry, payment processing, aging, and reconciliation to the General Ledger.

Typical responsibilities:

- Record vendor invoices (manual vouchers and PO-based invoices)
- Match invoices to receipts and POs where applicable
- Process payments (checks, ACH, wires)
- Reconcile vendor balances and GL control accounts
