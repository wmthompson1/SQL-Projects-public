# Working with Recurring Payables

Recurring payables automate invoices for regular charges (rent, subscriptions, maintenance).

Setup steps:

1. Create a recurring payables template: specify vendor, frequency, amount or calculation method, and GL distribution.
2. Assign start/end dates and any exceptions (skip dates, pro-rations).
3. Test-run the generation for a short period to verify amounts and distributions before scheduling a production run.

Maintenance:

- Review templates quarterly to ensure rates and GL distributions remain accurate.
- Archive templates no longer in use to avoid accidental generation.
