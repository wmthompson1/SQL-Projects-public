# Calculating Freight from Receipts

Freight charges may be captured on receipts or provided separately by vendors. This page describes common methods to calculate and apply freight.

Approaches:

- **Receipt-based freight**: Freight is captured on the receipt line and flows into the invoice when creating A/P from receipts.
- **Separate freight line**: Enter freight as a separate invoice line and assign the appropriate GL freight account.
- **Calculated freight**: Use freight rules (per weight, per piece, percentage of item cost) if your system supports freight calculation templates.

Steps:

1. Determine whether freight was recorded on the PO/Receipt or must be added manually.
2. If calculated, apply the freight rule to compute the freight amount and add as a voucher line.
3. Verify GL distribution for freight and any tax implications.

Notes:

- Keep freight policies consistent across receiving and AP teams to avoid mismatches.
- If freight is billed separately by vendors, ensure matching by reference number or shipment ID.
