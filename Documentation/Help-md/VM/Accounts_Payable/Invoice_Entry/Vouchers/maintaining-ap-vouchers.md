# Maintaining A/P Vouchers

This page describes common maintenance tasks for vouchers before posting to the ledger.

Common maintenance tasks:

- **Edit header fields** — update vendor references, invoice date, due date, and currency prior to posting.
- **Adjust line distributions** — correct GL accounts, cost centers, or job numbers on line distributions.
- **Attach supporting documents** — add scanned invoices, packing slips, or approval notes to the voucher record if your system supports attachments.
- **Resolve exceptions** — if a voucher was created from a receipt but price/quantity mismatches exist, document resolution steps and adjust amounts or quantities.

Safety notes:

- Do not modify posted vouchers without following your month-end or reversal policy; posting usually creates GL impact.
- Record the reason for material changes in the `Notes` or `Audit` fields for traceability.
