# Voiding an A/P Voucher

Voiding a voucher removes its payable effect while preserving audit information. Follow controls and accounting guidance.

Steps:

1. Confirm no payments have been issued against the voucher. If payments exist, reverse or reassign them first.
2. Verify the voucher is in an open accounting period; some systems block voids for closed periods.
3. Use the `Void Voucher` action or set the voucher status to `Voided`, add a reason in the `Notes`, and save.
4. If the voucher was already posted, follow your reversal procedure which may include a reversing voucher or adjusting journal entries.

Audit considerations:

- Maintain an audit note with user, date, and reason for voiding.
- Reconciliations should be updated to reflect the void; verify AP aging and GL control accounts after the void.
