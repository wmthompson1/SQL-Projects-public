# Activities in A/P Invoice Entry

Common activities and quick checks:

- **Create Voucher** — enter header and lines, attach receipts or supporting documents, and save as Draft.
- **Match Receipts** — link receipts to vouchers to prevent double-payment and to support three-way matching controls.
- **Approve Voucher** — move vouchers to an authorized approver or approval queue when required.
- **Generate Payments** — select approved vouchers and produce payment proposals, then create payment files or checks.
- **Post Batch** — post the payment batch or voucher postings to GL and confirm control account reconciliation.

Quick checks:

- Validate vendor banking details and payment terms before issuing payments.
- Reconcile unapplied credits and exceptions regularly to reduce AP aging issues.
