# What is A/P Invoice Entry?

A/P Invoice Entry is the module used to record, review, and maintain vendor invoices (vouchers). It supports invoice creation from PO receipts, manual voucher entry, matching, and preparation for payment.

Core functions:

- Manual voucher entry and editing
- Generating invoices from receipts and POs
- Managing voucher distributions and attachments
- Preparing vouchers for payment and posting to GL
