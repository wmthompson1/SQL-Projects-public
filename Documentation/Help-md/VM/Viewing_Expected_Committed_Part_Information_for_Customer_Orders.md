# Viewing Expected/Committed Part Information for Customer Orders

Expected/Committed C/O part information is two levels
down the Part Availability tree.

1. In the Part Availability
   window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window.
   The structure expands to the next level.
2. Highlight Expected/Committed
   and then click the plus box next to it. Double-click Expected/Committed
   to avoid clicking the plus box. The structure again expands.
3. Highlight Expected/Committed
   C/O.

Customer orders for that part appear in
the right pane. You can maximize the window and view the detail information
in the right pane or click the Committed CO plus box to expand the
tree to the next level, where the detail information appears in both
panes.

The Detail information appears in the right
pane as follows:

Demand ID - The
ID of the customer order.

Date - The date
on which the customer placed the order.

Quantity - The
quantity of the customer order line.

Unallocated Demand
- The quantity, if any, that you have yet to allocate to the customer
order to meet its quantitative requirements.
