# Modifying Customer Information on the fly

You can click the Customer Maintenance
button to view customer information for the current customer and make
any necessary changes.

1. Using the Customer Name
   or Customer ID button, select the customer for whom you are placing
   the order.
2. Click the Customer
   Maintenance button.

The Customer Maintenance window appears,
displaying the current customers information.

3. Modify any customer
   information.
4. When you are finished
   making changes, click the Save button.
