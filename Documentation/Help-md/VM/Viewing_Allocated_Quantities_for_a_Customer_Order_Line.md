# Viewing Allocated Quantities for a Customer Order Line

After either you or VISUAL assign supply to a C/O
line, the source of supply appears in the Supply Type column in the
VISUAL Order Management Customer Order Entry Window line item table.

## Viewing VISUAL Assignments of Supply from the Customers Primary Warehouse

If VISUAL has successfully assigned supply from the primary warehouse
available quantity, an I (Inventory) appears
in the Supply Type column. Also, the Allocate Quantity column contains
the whole order quantity - meaning that the orders quantity requirements
have been met.

## Viewing Your Assignments of Supply

If another two character entry - CP, PD, PO, WH, WO - or Multiple
Links appears in the Supply Type column, they indicates that
you have made the allocation (soft allocated) supply to the line using
the Assign Supply dialog box and accompanying allocation features.
