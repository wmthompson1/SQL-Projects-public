# What are Allocation Utilities?

The VISUAL Allocation Utility performs soft allocations
of supply quantities to customer order demand in a "batch mode."
Use the Allocation Utility to establish allocations, or "links,"
of supply to customer orders by part and/or warehouse and cut dramatically
the time you would normally spend in Customer Order Entry looking
for suitable links of supply for customer orders, one line at a time.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, set up
allocations by site.
