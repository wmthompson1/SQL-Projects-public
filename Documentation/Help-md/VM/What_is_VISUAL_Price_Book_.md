# What is VISUAL Price Book?

VISUAL Price Book is an add-on module that integrates
with Infor VISUALs order management system and other ERP pricing
packages. Infor VISUAL Price Book maintains its pricing definitions
in a separate database from the ERP database, with pricing derivations
available through an open COM interface, allowing Infor VISUAL Price
Book to be a centralized pricing solution.

Although there are several "standard" pricing methods
within various industries, VISUAL Price Book allows for any level
of "creative pricing" used in determining prices, discounts,
commissions, and order terms.

In addition to the pricing and discount styles currently supported
by Infor VISUAL (by customer, part, and quantity ordered), VISUAL
Price Book supports the following:

* Promotional pricing
* Part combination pricing
  (such as "buy one, get one free")
* Pricing based on accumulated
  sales
* Regional pricing
* Cost-based pricing
* Markup and markdown
* Across-the-board discounts
  and uplifts
* Price effectivity
* Best pricing optimization
