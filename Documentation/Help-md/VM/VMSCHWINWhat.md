# What is the Scheduling Window?

The Scheduling Window lets you view the shop schedule
relative to firmed work orders and work orders released to the floor.
Its flexibility gives you the ability to view multiple resources and
even multiple schedules at the same time. If you are using the Global
Scheduler, this window is a must for viewing the results of the schedule.

Three major reasons for creating a schedule are to understand how
much of your capacity is spoken for already, to identify possible
future threats to on-time deliveries, and to help your production,
procurement and sales departments to work together more successfully.
Because both load and capacity are dimensions of a schedule, VISUAL
provides the ability to have many different schedules. This allows
you to simulate any number of load/capacity scenarios. The production
schedule is treated as the real schedule for material planning and
work dispatching purposes.
