# Listing Payments

Use payment listing and inquiry screens to review receipts, reconcile payments to invoices, and investigate unapplied funds.

Key actions:

1. Open the Payments or Receipts inquiry and filter by `Customer ID`, `Date Range`, or `Batch ID`.
2. Review payment methods, reference numbers, and applied invoices.
3. For unreconciled payments, open the payment detail to apply it to outstanding invoices or move it to an unapplied payment queue.

Reconciliation guidance:

- Reconcile payments daily to reduce unapplied payment aging.
- Use export tools to extract payments for bank reconciliation when needed.
