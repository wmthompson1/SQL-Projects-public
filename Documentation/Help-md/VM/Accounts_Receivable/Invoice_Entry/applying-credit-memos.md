# Applying Credit Memos

Credit memos reduce customer balances and can be applied to open invoices or left as unapplied credits.

Steps to apply a credit memo:

1. Create the credit memo in `Invoice Entry` or the Credit Memo function, specifying `Customer ID`, memo amount, and reference.
2. Save the memo; it will appear as an unapplied credit if not immediately applied.
3. To apply it, open the target invoice and use `Apply Credit` or the payments/allocation screen to allocate the credit memo to the invoice amount.
4. Save the allocation; ensure the `Balance Due` decreases accordingly.

Notes:

- Some customers may require a refund instead of application; follow your refund procedures for issuing cash/back to the customer.
- Track credit memos for reporting and ensure they are included in customer statements.
