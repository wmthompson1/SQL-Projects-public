# Activities in AR Invoice Entry

This page summarizes common activities users perform in Invoice Entry and quick checks for each.

Common activities:

- **Create Invoice** — enter header and detail lines, save, then optionally print or post.
- **Edit Invoice** — modify non-posted invoices; ensure you have the necessary permissions.
- **Apply Payment/Credit** — allocate payments or credit memos to invoice lines.
- **Void Invoice** — mark an invoice as voided when it should not be collectible.
- **Post Batch** — aggregate invoices into a batch and post to the General Ledger.

Quick checks:

- When creating invoices, confirm customer terms, tax settings, and GL distributions.
- Before posting, run a batch total report and cross-check with AR trial balance.
