# Viewing Locked Part Information

Locked part information is one level down the Part
Availability tree.

1. In the Part Availability
   window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window.
   The structure expands to the next level.
2. Highlight Locked.

Detail information appears in the right
pane as follows:

Available to Customer
- The quantity of the part that is currently available to a customer.

Available - The
quantity that is currently available to assign to a customer order.
VISUAL determines this value by subtracting the Committed
quantity of the part from the On Hand quantity
of the part.

On Hand - The
quantity of the part that you have in a warehouse or warehouse locations.

Committed - The
portion of the On Hand quantity that is committed to a customer order,
work order material requirement and interbranch transfer.

Expected - The
quantity of parts that you expect to receive from work orders, purchase
orders, and interbranch transfers.

Expected/Committed
- The quantity of the part that is expected or committed through supply
allocation or demand allocation of customer orders, work order material
requirements, and interbranch transfers.

On Hold - The
quantity of the part that is on hold.

Locked - The quantity
of the part in inventory that you have locked. When you lock a part
in inventory, you must lock the entire inventory quantity in that
warehouse location. You cannot lock specified quantities.

Unavailable -
The quantity of the part that is unavailable to you to use for a customer
order.

Minimum Inventory
- The minimum quantity of the part that you must keep in inventory.

Unallocated Demand
- The quantity of the part that you have not allocated to demand.
