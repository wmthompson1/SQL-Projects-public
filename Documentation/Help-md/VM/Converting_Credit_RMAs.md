# Converting Credit RMAs

You can convert a Credit RMA to a Replace or Repair RMA. These conditions
apply to the conversions:

* If you created a new order
  during the Credit RMA process and received the returned materials,
  you must use this new order during the Replace or Repair RMA process.
* When you convert a Credit
  RMA to a Replace RMA, no additional processing occurs. If you
  created a new customer order for the Credit RMA, this customer
  order must be used to ship the replacement parts.
* When you convert a Credit
  RMA, you may need to re-open the customer order to process the
  repair.
