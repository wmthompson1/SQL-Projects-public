# Deleting Line Items

You can delete any line items that
do not have shipments against them.

1. With the desired customer
   open in the Customer Order Entry window, highlight the line item
   that you want to delete.
2. Click the Delete
   button.

An X appears in the far left column next
to the item you selected.

3. Click the Save
   button to remove the item and update the customer order.
