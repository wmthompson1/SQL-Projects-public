# What are the Order Management Child Windows?

The Order Management Window allows you to view information
regarding the current part, customer order, and sources of potential
supply that could help satisfy the quantitative needs of an order.
From the Order Management Window, you can open four child windows
that allow you total access to all aspects of a customer order.

From the Order Management Window, you can view:

* Part Information (Part Information
  child window)
* Customer Order History (Customer
  Order History child window
* Shipments (Shipments child
  window)
* Order Revision History
* WIP/VAS (WIP/VAS child window)

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, the child windows display
information for all of your sites.
