# Department Codes

Use the Department Maintenance table to create and
maintain Department IDs.

In a standard VISUAL database, departments represent the individual
areas that you use in your facility.

In a Project/A&D database, use departments to track actual costs
and operational budgets. Because the Infor VISUAL General Ledger system
must have the appropriate departments and prime accounts in order
for you to capture your actual costs and operational budgets, the
departments you use in your projects must also exist in the G/L. Departments,
along with Cost Categories, determine where VISUAL charges your costs
in your projects and general ledger.
