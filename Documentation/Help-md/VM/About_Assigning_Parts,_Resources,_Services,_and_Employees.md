# About Assigning Parts, Resources, Services, and Employees

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, you can
assign existing parts, shop resources, services, and employees to
your sites in the Site Maintenance window. Parts, resources, and services
must be assigned to a site before they can be used in transactions
for that site. Employees must be assigned to a site before they can
be used in a labor ticket.

If you are licensed to use a single site, all parts, shop resources,
and services automatically exist in your single site.
