# Viewing Information about Existing Purchase Orders for Material Requirements

You can view information about purchase orders linked
to material requirements directly in the Material Availability dialog
box. A purchase order is displayed in this dialog only if the purchase
order is formally linked to the material requirement.

To view purchase order information:

1. Select Info,
   Material Availability.
2. Select the line.
3. Select View,
   Purchase Order Links.
4. In the header, the totals
   for all purchase orders linked to the material requirement are
   displayed. This information is displayed:

Specification
- The specification from the material card for this requirement is
displayed.

Total Required
- The total quantity of part required for the operation is displayed.

Total Issued -
The total quantity of part issued to the operation is displayed.

Total Received
- The total quantity received for all purchase orders linked to the
requirement is displayed.

Stock U/M - The
unit used to measure the part for stocking purposes is displayed.

Total Allocated
- The total quantity that has been allocated to the operation from
all purchase orders is displayed.

Total Fulfilled
- The total linked quantity from all purchase orders that has been
fulfilled is displayed.

5. In the line item table,
   information about individual linked purchased orders is displayed.
   This information is displayed:

Order ID - The
ID of the purchase order is displayed.

Ln# - The purchase
order line number linked to the material requirement is displayed.

Vendor ID - The
vendor specified on the purchase order is displayed.

Vendor Part ID
- The ID that the vendor uses for the ordered part is displayed.

Order Qty - The
quantity of part ordered on the line is displayed.

Recv Qty - The
quantity of part received into inventory is displayed.

Status - The current
status of the purchase order is displayed.

Purchase U/M -
The unit used to measure the part for purchasing purposes is displayed.

P/O Alloc'd Qty
- The quantity of part on the purchase order line allocated for the
material requirement is displayed.

P/O Fulfilled Qty
- The allocated quantity of part that has been received is displayed.

Link Alloc'd Qty
- The total quantity allocated to the material card is displayed.

Link Received Qty
- The total allocated quantity that has been received is displayed.

Link Issued Qty
- The total allocated quantity that has been issued to the work order
is displayed.

6. To open the purchase
   order, double-click the Purchase Order ID.
