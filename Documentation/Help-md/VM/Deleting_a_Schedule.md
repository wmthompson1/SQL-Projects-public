# Deleting a Schedule

1. Highlight the schedule
   you want to delete by clicking in the left-most column of the
   Schedule ID table.
2. Click the Delete
   button.

An X appears in the left-most column on
the table next to the selected line.

3. Click the Save
   button to complete the deletion.
