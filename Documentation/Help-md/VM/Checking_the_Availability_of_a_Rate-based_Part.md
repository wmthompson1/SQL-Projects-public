# Checking the Availability of a Rate-based Part

When you check the availability of a rate-based part
in Customer Order Entry or the Manufacturing Window, this additional
information is displayed:

Rate per Day The quantity that the work
order produces each day.

First Delivery Date The first day that
the part is scheduled to be produced based on the schedule input settings.
Note that the quantity produced on the first delivery date could be
less than the daily rate. If the total quantity produced in the work
order is not a multiple of the daily rate, then a quantity that is
less than the daily quantity is produced on the first day. For example,
if the daily rate of a part is 8 and the total quantity of the work
order is 20, then 4 parts are produced on the first day.
