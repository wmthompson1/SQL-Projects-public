# Top Customers by Revenue Detail

This [dashboard](VMDBOARDWhat.md) detail
grid lists the invoices for the customers with the highest total invoice
amounts. You can specify the number of customers to include.

Date filter applies to: Invoice Date

Filters: Sales Rep ID, Territory, Product
Code, Commodity Code, Site ID

Red text: If the Balance Amount is red,
the invoice is overdue.

Details: Customer ID, Customer Name, Invoice
Date, Due Date, Last Paid Date, Total Amount, Discount Given, Paid
Amount, Balance Amount, Sales Rep, Site ID.
