# Starting Outside Service Maintenance

Service Maintenance is available from the Infor VISUAL
Main window.

To access the Service Maintenance window:

Select Outside Service Maintenance from the Eng/Mfg
menu.

The Service Maintenance window appears with no information in it.

The Service Maintenance window contains entry fields, buttons, and
tabs.
