# What are Intrastat and ESL Reports?

Use Intrastat and ESl reports to organize your data
into a format that meets your requirements.

By making various selections, you can tailor the report to better
meet the specific needs of the report on which you are working.

You can Print, View,
and Export your data from the report window.
