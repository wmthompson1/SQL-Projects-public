# Calendar

![Calendar1.gif](Graphics/Calendar.png "Calendar1.gif")

A calendar for the current month appears.

Select a date by double clicking on it.

To scroll ahead to future months, click the right arrow.

To scroll back to previous months, click the left arrow.

When you select a date, the calendar closes. The date you have chosen
appears in the date field.
