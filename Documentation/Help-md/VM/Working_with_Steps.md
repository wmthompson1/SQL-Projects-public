# Working with Steps

When working with step properties, you may want to
take some time to investigate and think about what you want to achieve
with the step. Some of the things you may want to consider are:

* What are you attempting
  to do with this step?
* What conditions do you want
  met?
* Are there any errors on
  which you want to halt the process? Do you want to notify the
  user on these errors but allow a continuation of the process?
* Are there any documents
  you want attached to this step of the process?
* Are there any people you
  want to notify at this step?
* Because what happens on
  one step or rule can effect other steps, you may also want to
  consider what happens on prior and subsequent rules and steps.
