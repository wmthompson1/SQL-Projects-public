# Viewing Month-to-Date and Year-to-Date Customer Status

You can view month-to-date and year-to-date order
information for your customers in Customer Inquiry.

1. Call up any customer
   order for which you want to check month-to-date and year-to-date
   order information into the Customer Inquiry information window.
2. Select Show
   Customer MTD/YTD Status from the Info menu.

A dialog box appears, listing the value
ordered each month from the beginning of the year to the present date
and the year-to-date value ordered.

3. When you have finished
   viewing the information, click the Close
   button.

You return to the Customer Inquiry information
window.

4. Click the Close
   button to return to the Customer Inquiry window.
