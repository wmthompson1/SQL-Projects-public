The following VISUAL applications support macros:

* Customer Order Entry
* Customer Maintenance
* Estimating Window
* Purchase Order Entry
* Purchase Receipt Entry
* Purchase Requisition Entry
* Vendor Maintenance
* Vendor RFQ Entry
* ECN Entry
* Interbranch Transfer Entry
* Interbranch Shipping Entry
* Interbranch Receipt Entry
* Order Management Window
* Return Material Authorization
* Manufacturing Window, at both
  the header level and material/operation level
