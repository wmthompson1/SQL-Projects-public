# Viewing Available Part Quantities

Available to customer quantity information is also
one level down the Part Availability tree.

1. In the Part Availability
   window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window.
   The structure expands to the next level.
2. Highlight Available.
   The Detail information that appears in the right pane is the same
   as the information for Available to Customer.
