# Detailing Quantities

Select Detail Quantities from the View menu to view all of the quantity columns.
Deselecting this option hides the Total Quantity Required, Quantity
Issued, Quantity Ordered, and Quantity Received columns. This simplifies
viewing by only showing the net required and net due quantities.
