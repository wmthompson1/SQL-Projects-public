# Using Specifications and Notations

Specifications and Notations are tools that allow
you to attach quantities of free-form text to Infor VISUAL Financials
objects.

Specifications allow you to enter a block of text to associate with
an order, line item, or other object. These are generally meant to
convey information about the order itself, to be used in production
and communicated to the customer or vendor.

You can associate specifications with orders and invoices.

Notations (or notes, for short) allow you to keep internal notes
about customers and invoices. They allow a running dialog or commentary
where each separate entry is date and time stamped. Notations are
purely an internal tool, and do not appear on any outgoing paperwork.
In this way, they are different from specs.

You can associate notations with orders and invoices, customers
and vendors.
