# Printing Barcode Labels

The ability to Print Barcode Labels is fundamental
to the VISUAL BTS system. Without proper barcode label identification,
all of the functions of VISUAL BTS would be useless. These functions
are performed by the VISUAL BTS-LP module through the use of multiple
interfaces. Each of these functions allow printing of the assorted
label types from their respective areas. Each general label type will
be available for print from their respective functions within the
standard Infor VISUAL windows.

## Received / Raw Materials Labels

Raw Material Labels are a method for marking any material with barcode
identification. They provide a basic means for identifying materials
and inventory. These labels can be printed upon receipt of a purchase
order or as a means for labeling any material.

## Parts Labels

Parts Labels are a method for marking any parts with barcode identification.
They provide a basic means for identifying inventory. These labels
can be printed upon request as a means for labeling any part in inventory.

## Part Traceability Labels

Part Traceability Labels are a method for marking any parts requiring
Traceability with barcode identification. They provide a basic means
for identifying traceable inventory. These labels can be printed upon
request as a means for labeling any traceable part in inventory.

## Finished Goods Labels

Finished Goods Labels are applied as a means to identify the goods
that are marked for sale or consumption after receipt from a workorder.
They provide a distinct identification that has different data sources
as compared to Raw Material or Parts Labels.

## Shipping Labels

Shipping Labels are designed to satisfy shipping requirements of
customers or freight shippers. This form of identification provides
a barcoded tracking of finished goods as they exit the facilities.

## Warehouse / Locations Labels

Warehouse / Locations Labels provide a method to mark the storage
facilities with barcodes for scanning when materials are moved within
the facility.
