# Designating Legs and Engineering Masters as Eligible for Updating

Before you can use the master updater to update masters from legs,
you must designate masters as eligible for updating.  To designate
an engineering master as eligible for updating from legs:

1. Open the engineering
   master.
2. Open the header card.
3. Click the Engineering
   tab.
4. Select the Allow
   Update from Leg check box.
5. Click Save.

If the engineering master has legs, then you need to designate each
leg as eligible for updating. Open the leg header card, click the
Engineering tab, and select the Allow Update from Master check box.

Before you can use the leg updater to update legs from masters,
you must designate legs as eligible for updating. To designate a leg
as eligible for updating from an engineering master:

1. Open the work order,
   quote master, or engineering master.
2. Open the header card
   of the leg.
3. Click the Engineering
   tab.
4. Select the Allow
   Update from Master check box.
5. Click Save.
