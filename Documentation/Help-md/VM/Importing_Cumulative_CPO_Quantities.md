# Importing Cumulative CPO Quantities

When you are importing your CPO documents, you have
a choice of Discrete or Cumulative quantities.

When looking at the Cumulative examples, keep in mind the following:

* Set the Subtract Intransit
  Quantity on the header section of your CPO layout. You can also
  set the Subtract Intransit Quantity in the Options>Allocate
  Delivery Schedule section.
* If you are working with
  Subtract Intransit Quantities, refer to [Working
  with Subtract Intransit Quantities](Working_with_Subtract_Intransit_Quantities.htm) for more information.
