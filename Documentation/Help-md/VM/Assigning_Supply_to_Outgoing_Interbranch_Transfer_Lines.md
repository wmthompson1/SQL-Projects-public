# Assigning Supply to Outgoing Interbranch Transfer Lines

VISUAL automatically assigns supply to outgoing IBT
lines from available inventory quantities in the "From"
warehouse, or the warehouse from which the transfer originates. However,
if VISUAL does not find sufficient supply, or fails to hard allocate
the required quantity, you can "soft" allocate supply from
other sources, or borrow against future quantities that you expect
to receive. When you soft allocate to demand, you assign unreceived
but entered and confirmed quantities from purchase order lines, purchase
order delivery schedules, warehouse inventory, work orders quantities,
co-products, and incoming transfers to the outgoing line at hand to
meet its quantitative requirements.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, you can allocate supply
from sources within the site associated with the From warehouse only.
