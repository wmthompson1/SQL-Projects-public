# Viewing Expected Part Information for Interbranch Transfers

You can view information regarding parts that you
expect from interbranch transfers two levels down on the Part Availability
tree.

1. In the Part Availability
   window, double-click the icon in the left pane of the window.
   The structure expands to the next level.
2. Highlight Expected
   and then click the plus box next to it. Double-click Expected
   to avoid clicking the plus box. The structure again expands.
3. Highlight Expected
   IBT. Interbranch transfers for that part appear in the
   right pane. You can maximize the window and view the detail information
   in the right pane or click the Expected IBT
   plus box to expand the tree to the next level, where the detail
   information appears in both panes.

The Detail information that appears in the
right pane as follows:

Supply ID - The
ID of the IBT.

Date - The date
on which you initiated the IBT.

Order Quantity
- The quantity of the IBT.

Received - The
quantity of the IBT that you have received into inventory.

Allocated - The
portion of the IBT quantity that you have allocated to demand.

Unallocated Demand
- The quantity, if any, that you have yet to allocate from the IBT
to demand.
