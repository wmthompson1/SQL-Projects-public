# Deleting a Sales Tax Group

You can delete tax groups from the database, but only
if no customer orders are associated with the tax group you are deleting.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| WARNING.gif | Deleting a Sales Tax Group permanently removes the information from the database. You can NOT recover deleted tax group information. |

To delete a tax group from the database:

1. Call the tax group you
   want to delete to the Tax Group Maintenance window and click the
   Delete button.

A dialog box appears, asking you to confirm
the deletion.

2. Click Yes
   to complete the deletion. Click No to
   cancel.

The Sales Tax Group Maintenance window still
contains the tax group information, but the tax group is no longer
in the database.

3. Click the Clear
   button to complete the operation.
