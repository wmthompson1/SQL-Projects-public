# Scrolling in the Manufacturing Window

You can use the scroll bars at the bottom and at the left of the
Manufacturing Window to scroll to view information.

In the Graphical and Text displays, you can also click and drag
the window to scroll. Place your cursor in a blank area of the window
(an area that does not contain any object cards). Click the mouse
button. The cursor changes to a hand. With your mouse button depressed,
drag the mouse to scroll the information in the window.

If you are using the two-window option, you can drag the bottom
window only. You cannot drag the window where the material requirements
are displayed.
