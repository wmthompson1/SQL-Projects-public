# Customer Accepts Partial Shipments Setting and Consolidated Sales

To ship a consolidated sales order to a customer,
the customer must accept partial shipments. In Customer Maintenance,
ensure that the Customer Accepts Partial Shipments check box on the
Shipping tab is selected for customers to whom you are shipping consolidated
sales orders.
