# Resetting Outside Service Costs in the Costed Bill of Material Report Window

When you reset outside service costs, costs in engineering
masters, quote masters, and work orders are updated based on the costs
that are specified for services in Outside Service Maintenance.

You can also reset material requirement costs in Outside Service
Maintenance.

1. Select Eng/Mfg,
   Costed Bill of Material Report.
2. Select Edit,
   Reset Service Costs.
3. Specify the types of
   masters to update:
4. To update
   engineering masters, select the Engineering
   Master operations check box.
5. To update
   work orders, select the Work Order operations
   check box.
6. To update
   quotes, select the Quote Master operations
   check box.

4. If you selected the
   Work Order operations check box, you
   can reset service costs based on the status of each operation.
   Select the According to operation status
   check box and select the statuses to use:

* Unreleased
* Firmed
* Released
* Closed
* Cancelled
* All

5. Select the According
   to W/O or Master creation date check box, and then use
   the On or after and On or before fields to specify the create
   date of the masters that you are updating. To update all masters,
   specify today's date in the On or before field and leave the On
   or after field blank.
6. Click Ok.
