# Working with Toolbars and Views

Toolbars and menus make accessing additional functionality
easier.

## Management Toolbar

Use the Management toolbar to set Starting and Ending
date ranges for the information appearing in your child windows.

You can also save Purchase Management Window views enabling you
to open sets of views for your convenience. For example, you open
the Part Demand, History, and Management child windows and drag your
parts into the Purchase Order Entry child window. You can save this
specific set of views by entering a name in the View field and clicking
the Save toolbar button. You can then open
your saved set of views, regardless of which view you currently have
open, by selecting your saved name and clicking the refresh button.
