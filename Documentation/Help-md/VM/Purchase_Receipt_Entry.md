# Purchase Receipt Entry

The receipts of raw materials on a Purchase Order
are a fundamental transaction that provides an excellent starting
point for all the material barcode transactions. Any material correctly
labeled can be easily received into the facility and the status of
the Purchase Order is instantly updated. The flow of this transaction
is to allow a materials handler to sufficiently receive materials
and update the quantities of a purchase order from paperwork or the
package labels.

The chain of events is similar to the following:

* Materials arrive at the
  receiving area
* (Optional) Raw Material
  Labels are printed and applied to the received materials
* Purchase Order is determined
  and scanned
* Material is scanned to relieve
  Purchase Order
* Material is Located (and
  location is scanned)
