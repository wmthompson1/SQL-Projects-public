# What Are Repair RMAs?

Use Repair RMAs when your company has agreed to receive the materials,
diagnose and repair them, then reship them to the customer. Depending
on the way you schedule the repairs, this may involve a work order.
The new work order tracks the value being added to the part, not the
original cost of the material.
