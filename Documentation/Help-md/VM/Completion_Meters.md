# Completion Meters

A horizontal bar appears to the left of each line
in text mode, and at the top of each card in graphical mode.

This horizontal bar indicates the percentage of the total completion
for the object. It begins empty, and fills as work progresses.

For the header, the meter shows the total completion of the project.
For tasks, legs, and materials, the meter shows the completion status
of the individual object.
