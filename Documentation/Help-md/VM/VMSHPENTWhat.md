# What is Shipping Entry?

The Shipping Entry application is for Shipping and
Receiving personnel to ship customer orders and process returns. Shipping
Entry provides the following functions:

* Records shipment of materials
  ordered by a customer through Customer Order Entry, including
  ship date, quantity shipped vs. quantity ordered, and actual freight
  charges.
* Process returns of previously
  shipped materials.
* Prints shipping paperwork.
* Performs automatic inventory
  transactions to issue material to orders, or return material to
  stock.

In performing these functions, Shipping Entry creates a Packlist,
sometimes referred to as a Shipper. A Packlist is VISUAL object that
is linked to a Customer Order and records shipment and return dates
and quantities. It is important to realize when viewing the Shipping
Entry window that some of the data is part of the Packlist, and some
is being displayed from the associated Customer Order.

The Shipping Entry application works with orders created in Customer
Order Entry. You may wish to familiarize yourself with that application.
An understanding of inventory transactions will also be helpful.

If you are licensed to use multiple sites, ship orders on a site-by-site
basis. The site that ships orders to customers must be the same site
in which the order was placed.
