# ShopResources

```sql
SELECT SRS.RESOURCE_ID
    , SRS.USER_1
    , SRS.SETUP_COST_PER_HR
    , SRS.RUN_COST_PER_HR
    , SRS.BUR_PER_HR_SETUP
    , SRS.BUR_PER_HR_RUN
    , SRS.BUR_PERCENT_SETUP
    , SRS.BUR_PERCENT_RUN
    , SRS.AFC_GL_ACCT_ID
    , SRS.AFB_GL_ACCT_ID
FROM SHOP_RESOURCE_SITE SRS 
WHERE SRS.STATUS <> 'O' OR STATUS IS NULL
ORDER BY SRS.USER_1

```
